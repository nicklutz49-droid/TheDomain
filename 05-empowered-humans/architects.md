# Architects

Architects are humans who learn to interact with the
[Domain](../02-the-domain/the-domain.md) through study and mathematical
understanding. They earn abilities through decades of disciplined work.

## Training

Schools provide foundational learning in Domain theory,
[Flux](../02-the-domain/flux.md) manipulation, and mathematics. **Full mastery takes
20–30 years.** The Union's academies run 10–15 year programs with a 40% graduation
rate; the Confederacy's major training center is Resonance City. See
[Education](../08-civilization/education.md).

## Specializations

- **Combat Architects** — offensive and defensive Flux applications
- **Medical Architects** — biological manipulation and healing (systematized by [Yumeko Tanaka](legendary-figures/yumeko-tanaka.md))
- **Civil Engineers** — construction, infrastructure, terrain modification
- **Theoretical Architects** — study the Domain itself
- **Glitch Specialists** — predict and contain Hollow manifestations
- **Relay Technicians** — maintain and repair sacred infrastructure

## Architect technology

Architects build devices that channel Domain capabilities through **Flux
Circuitry** — physical patterns inscribed into materials that direct energy flow.

Key technologies: airships, trains, holo-comms, voice boxes, healing matrices,
diagnostic arrays, prosthetics, reality locks, containment devices, Flux weapons.
See [Technology](../08-civilization/technology.md).

## The limits of understanding

Architects know which patterns produce which effects through accumulated tradition.
**They don't know why patterns work.** The underlying mechanism remains opaque —
they recognize [god-script](../02-the-domain/god-script.md) patterns without
understanding the language.

*(Restricted framing: an Architect shaping Flux is unknowingly submitting requests
to the Domain's systems. Their "tradition" is a cargo-cult API.)*
