# Altheus the Unbroken

Earth-affinity [Fluxborn](../fluxborn.md). Stood alone at a mountain pass for three
days against a [Hollow](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/hollow/_index.md) incursion.

## Legacy

The **Altheus Medal** is the [Union's](../../07-geography/federal-union.md) highest
military honor.

## Continuity note

His two source dates conflict, and the earlier one predates the first Fluxborn
under one of the two Entity-awakening dates — resolving him resolves both issues.
See [continuity notes](../../00-meta/continuity-notes.md).
