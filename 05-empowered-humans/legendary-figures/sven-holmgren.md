# Architect-General Sven Holmgren

Militarized [Architect](../architects.md) practice, ~400 years ago. Died in battle
against a **persistent [Hollow](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/hollow/persistent/_index.md)**.

## Legacy

The doctrine lineage from Holmgren runs to the Union's
[Architect Corps](../../09-power-and-conflict/military.md) and combat-Architect
practice everywhere.
