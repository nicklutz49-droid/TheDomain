# The Sealer — Dren Ashford

Led the team that sealed
[Malevictus](../../04-divine-beings/sealed-gods/malevictus.md), ~800 years ago — the
**first confirmed sealing by Architects without divine assistance**.

## Why it matters

Before Ashford, only the Twelve collectively could seal a god. After him, humans
hold that power too — which changes every negotiation between mortals and gods.
