# Lady Architect Yumeko Tanaka

Developed systematic medical [Architect](../architects.md) practice, ~1,200 years
ago.

## The choice

She was a [Fluxborn](../fluxborn.md) who **chose to suppress her abilities** and
learn the Architect way instead — the purest embodiment of the setting's
gift-versus-understanding theme.
