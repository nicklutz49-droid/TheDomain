# Fluxborn

Fluxborn are humans gifted with direct Domain connections by
[the Entity](../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md) *(GM-only: no one in-world knows the
gifter's identity)*. Unlike [Architects](architects.md) who spend decades learning,
Fluxborn receive abilities fully formed. The first Fluxborn appeared approximately
1,800 years ago, when the Entity achieved consciousness. *(Dating conflict noted in
[continuity notes](../00-meta/continuity-notes.md).)*

## Power Affinities

Abilities are determined by core personality and values. **Element is fixed;
expression varies.**

| Core Value | Element | Manifestations |
|---|---|---|
| Freedom | Air/Wind | Flight, intangibility, sound manipulation |
| Adaptability | Water | Shapeshifting, healing, fluid movement |
| Stability | Earth | Durability, strength, terrain control |
| Passion | Fire | Combustion, energy projection, heat control |
| Connection | Lightning | Telepathy, technopathy, enhanced speed |
| Knowledge | Light | Illusions, revelation, enhanced perception |
| Secrecy | Shadow | Invisibility, memory manipulation, stealth |
| Order | Metal/Crystal | Construction, barriers, binding |
| Chaos | Void | Dissolution, probability, nullification |

*(GM-only: the Entity's emphasis on **Chaos-affinity** Fluxborn is a weapon program
against [the Shriven](../03-hidden-truth/the-shriven.md), whose hyper-ordered
biology is vulnerable to Static Errors.)*

## The Gifting Process

- The Entity selects recipients through criteria no one understands — though it appears to favor certain family lines.
- The selection process begins with **dreams** — those who ignore or forget these dreams are not selected.
- Gifting occurs during sleep; recipients wake with abilities fully formed.

## Fluxborn in society

All three powers recruit Fluxborn aggressively. Most serve military or government
functions. Independent Fluxborn are rare and watched carefully — see
[Fluxborn for hire](../09-power-and-conflict/criminal-underworld.md). A single
powerful Fluxborn can turn a battle; the
[Fluxborn Accords](../09-power-and-conflict/military.md) govern their treatment as
prisoners (honored in spirit, violated in practice).

Fluxborn powers **fail** in deadzones like [the Silence](../07-geography/the-silence.md).
