# Deputy Archivist Velia Sorn

Thirty-eight; Deputy Archivist of the **Meridian Archives**; protégée of High
Curator [Valdris Crane](valdris-crane.md), whom she admires, and whose archive she
is unknowingly about to catch.

## The archivist

Sorn is the Archives' finder — the person summoned when a record *should* exist
and doesn't. Career built on an instinct she trusts completely: absences have
shapes. Genuinely apolitical, which is why her clearances kept rising; genuinely
curious, which is why that was a mistake.

## The thread she's pulling (restricted)

A routine provenance check led her to the
[Verantum Ascension](../01-foundation/events/the-verantum-ascension.md) holdings,
where she found the same *shaped absence* across independent collections —
corrections too consistent to be human error, too old to be recent fraud, in
archives that never shared custody. Her private file on it is titled, with
archivist humor, **"The Tidy Century."** She has not yet connected it to her
mentor's other life. She has noticed that a quiet, precise woman in reading-gloves
([Vesperine](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/vesperine.md), though Sorn doesn't
know it) keeps requesting adjacent boxes.
