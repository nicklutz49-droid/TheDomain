# Sister Amaranth — the Backward Saint

*(Canon figure — the Backward Saint of the
[Testimonies](../10-supplementary/prophecies-and-sacred-texts.md); the name
"Amaranth" and all biography are draft.)*

## Canon basis

Someone experiencing time in reverse, whose recorded statements form the Backward
Saint's Testimonies. She has said she *"will be there when it all begins."*

## Expanded detail (draft)

- **The condition.** Amaranth experiences her life in the opposite order from everyone else — her yesterday is our tomorrow. Conversation with her is famously disorienting: she answers before questions, grieves people who are alive, and greets strangers like old friends because, for her, they are.
- **The keeping.** She lives in a small shrine-hospice outside Haven's Rest, tended by a rotating order of volunteer scribes — the **Amaranthine Watch** — who record everything she says and file it with the Dream Archive, per a treaty older than any current government. She is gentle, patient with her keepers ("they're always new, and always the same"), and increasingly *young-acting* as the years pass, which no one likes thinking about.
- **The Testimonies in practice:** useless for prediction on demand — she can't be interrogated forward — but devastating in retrospect: her filed statements have matched the [Night of Doors](../01-foundation/events/the-night-of-doors.md) ("forty-one guests, one kept, tell his sister *kept*"), the Pale Closure, and three assassinations, each unambiguous only after the fact.

## The terminal statement (restricted)

Her oldest filed statement — which, in her order, is the *last* thing she'll say —
is fixed and famous among her keepers: *"I will be there when it all begins."*
Recent statements have started orbiting it: mentions of a white corridor, of
"the month with no moon," of *"the seat that was saved for me."* The Amaranthine
Watch has not forwarded these to the Archive. They've started arguing about why.

## Open threads

- Backward from when? If her life runs in reverse, her *birth* lies in our future — plausibly around the [Great Alignment](../03-hidden-truth/the-shriven.md). "There when it all begins" may be simple autobiography. *Tale: [The Scribe's First Day](../11-story/tales/the-scribes-first-day.md).*
- "The seat that was saved for me" — the [Concordance's empty thirteenth seat](../01-foundation/events/the-concordance.md) suddenly has a claimant. Is she guessing, remembering, or joking? Her keepers can no longer tell.
