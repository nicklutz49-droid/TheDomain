# Ashka of the Pale

The only confirmed person to leave the
[Pale Sovereignty](../07-geography/pale-sovereignty.md) since the Closure — she
threaded the [Maze](../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/the-pale-maze.md) two
years ago and has been running ever since. *(This realizes the canon narrative
seed: "Someone escapes the Pale with knowledge of what Palladia is planning.")*

## Who she is

Pale-born, mid-twenties by appearance: the isolation phenotype in full —
paper-pale, tall, eyes made for low light, flinching at unfiltered noon. By
vocation a **relay-singer**: one of the Pale caste who coax its grown-technology
through tone and cadence. She carries a **seed-tool** — a palm-sized instrument
that heals when damaged — which is currently the only piece of Pale technology
outside the Maze.

## What she knows (restricted)

She won't say it all, and what she has said is bad enough:

- The Maze is *thinner* from inside — the Pale can see out better than the world can see in.
- [Palladia](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/palladia.md) is **counting down to something**. Public liturgy in the Pale now includes a number, recited daily, decreasing.
- Relay-singers were being reassigned, in growing numbers, to the drowned coast — to the sunken temple Palladia went to find the week the fog came.
- She left because her sister was reassigned there and came back *changed* — serene, tireless, and singing in her sleep in no language Ashka knows.

## Now

Hiding in [Crossroads](../07-geography/neutral-territories.md) under
[Breccia's](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/breccia.md) informal protection —
wayside gods notice who walks their roads gently. Hunted by Union intelligence
(politely), the [Cabal](../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-cabal.md)
(patiently), and something else: a **white bird** that is always in the corner of
her eye and never in the center, which she believes is Palladia's regard, stretched
thin across the Maze.

## Open threads

- The daily number: countdown to what — the barriers falling, or something Palladia means to finish *before* they do?
- The seed-tool answers Ashka's voice. It has recently begun, very quietly, answering on its own — a half-tone, at dusk, facing east. Toward the Pale, or toward the moon? Depends on the season, which is worse.
