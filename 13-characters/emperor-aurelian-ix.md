# Emperor Aurelian IX

Seventy-one years old, forty years on the throne, called **the Quiet Emperor** —
first with affection, lately with unease. Presides over the
[succession crisis](../09-power-and-conflict/politics/imperial-succession.md) that
will define the Empire's next age.

## The reign

Aurelian IX's genius was *maintenance*: forty years of no wars, careful
Wound-frontier funding, and Great Houses kept in balance by a man who read every
report and forgot nothing. The Empire under Aurelian didn't advance; it *held* —
which, his defenders note, is what [Mordrath](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/mordrath.md)
keeps saying matters.

## Now

His heart is failing faster than the court admits. Three heirs circle
(Darion, Elara, Lucan); the privy council manages his hours; and the Emperor has
begun doing something he never did in forty years — granting private audiences
out of protocol order, as if working down a personal list.

## Secret (restricted)

He petitioned [Kythera](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/kythera.md) to mend his
heart, in person, at her own altar. **She refused** — gently, and would not say
why. He has told no one what she *did* say, which was: *"Not yet. You'll
understand why."* He does not understand why, and it is consuming more of his
remaining strength than the heart is.
