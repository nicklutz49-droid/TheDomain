# Valdris Crane

High Curator of the Meridian Archives; leader of the
[Cabal](../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-cabal.md). *(Canon figure,
promoted from the Cabal file per vault conventions; draft additions marked.)*

## Canon basis

- Leads the most significant secret society on Earth from within the Union's greatest archive.
- Secretly a **Shadow-affinity [Fluxborn](../05-empowered-humans/fluxborn.md)**.
- His sister was killed by Hollow **while a god watched** — the wound the Cabal grew around.
- [Thessaly](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/thessaly.md) has known of him for decades and watches *with amusement*.

## Expanded detail (draft)

- **The public man:** donnish, generous with junior scholars, famous for never forgetting a citation. The Archives run better under Crane than under any curator in a century — cover so good it stopped being cover.
- **The god who watched** was never named in his hearing. He has spent forty years narrowing candidates by territory and date. The list is down to two. One of them is the patron of the province where he was born.
- **The irony he carries:** his own powers are the [Entity's](../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md) gift — the system he's dissecting *chose* him. He has considered, in his worst hours, that the choosing and his sister's death might be the same event viewed twice. He files the thought under "unverified" and keeps working.
- **Succession:** the Apex is five; Crane is past sixty. He is grooming no one, which the Inner Circle has noticed, and [Velia Sorn](velia-sorn.md) — brilliant, unwitting, incorruptible — keeps rising toward the wrong clearance.
