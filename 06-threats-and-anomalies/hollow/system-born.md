# System-Born Hollows (Class II–V)

Errors of the Domain's own machinery rather than human psychology.

## Geometrics (Class II–IV)
Impossible shapes causing nosebleeds. Must be **"solved" mathematically** —
Architect work.

## Null Entities (Class V)
Holes in reality. Everything they touch ceases to exist. **Divine intervention
required.**

## Static Swarms (Class III)
Particle clouds consuming energy — Flux, electricity, life force.

*(GM-only aside: "Static" is also the Shriven's known vulnerability — the Entity's
[Chaos-affinity program](../../03-hidden-truth/the-shriven.md) weaponizes what these
swarms do naturally. Whether that's convergence or design is an open thread.)*
