# Nightmare-Born Hollows (Class II–IV)

Manifestations shaped by human fear. *(The Dreamweaver ended a plague of these
~275 years ago — see [The Dreamweaver](../../05-empowered-humans/legendary-figures/the-dreamweaver.md).)*

## Dread Walkers (Class II)
Seven-foot humanoids with wrong proportions. Develop features of someone the victim
trusts. Vulnerable to bright light.

## The Weeping (Class II)
Children with hollow eyes. Appear closer each time you look away. Must be contained
using mirrored surfaces.

## Maw Crawlers (Class III)
Masses of teeth drawn to fear. Fire effective.

## Skinjackers (Class IV)
Possess living humans, consuming them slowly. **40% survival rate for removal.**

## The Faceless Beloved (Class IV)
Takes the form of someone loved — but the face is missing. Must be destroyed with
conviction.
