# Military-Scale Hollows (Class III–IV)

Threats that require armies, not hunters.

## Siege Maws (Class III)
Elephant-sized quadrupeds. Require twenty soldiers with Flux weapons or three combat
Fluxborn.

## The Procession (Class III)
A column of humanoids absorbing anyone in their path. Must destroy all
simultaneously.

## Brood Mothers (Class IV)
House-sized creatures producing dozens of offspring daily.

## Mirror Legions (Class IV)
Duplicates of whoever views them. **If a duplicate kills its original, it becomes
permanent.**
