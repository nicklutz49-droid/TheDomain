# Persistent Hollows (Class V)

Named, unique, and effectively permanent. Standard doctrine for all of them:
**do not engage.**

- [The Grandfather](the-grandfather.md) — bargains at the Wound's edge
- [The Choir](the-choir.md) — it collects heads
- [The Patient One](the-patient-one.md) — always behind you
- [The Garden](the-garden.md) — fifty square miles of single organism

## Restricted note

[Ithrazel](../../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/ithrazel.md) has *communicated* with
persistent Hollow entities and suspects they're fragments of something trying to
exist. [Architect-General Holmgren](../../../05-empowered-humans/legendary-figures/sven-holmgren.md)
died fighting one (~400 ya) — possibly one of these four, possibly one now gone or
undocumented.
