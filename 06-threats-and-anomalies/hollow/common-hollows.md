# Common Hollows (Class I)

Manageable by trained civilians.

## Flickers
Small humanoid shapes of stuttering light. Scavengers. Bright light makes them
fragile.

## Mumblers
Amorphous blobs that dampen sound. Easily destroyed.

## Dustlings
Animate dust clouds consuming organic matter slowly. Fire destroys them.

## Echoes
Translucent afterimages of deceased people. Psychological danger only.

## Gnawers
Rat-like creatures eating inorganic material. Standard pest control works.

## Crawling Hands
Severed hands crawling on fingers. Fragile.
