# The Wound

**Location:** Central Europe · **Type:** Reality collapse · **Threat:** Extreme

The largest glitch zone on Earth — approximately **400 miles across**, and
*consuming* central and eastern Europe. The defining terror of the
[Feudal Empire's](../../07-geography/feudal-empire/_index.md) eastern frontier.

## Known features

- [The Grandfather](../hollow/persistent/the-grandfather.md), a Class V persistent Hollow, offers bargains at its edge.
- [House Kravenhold](../../07-geography/feudal-empire/great-houses.md) holds the western frontier specifically to guard against Wound expansion.
