# Glitch Zones

Areas of permanently corrupted Domain activity. The master table from the source
(§20), reconstructed:

| Zone | Location | Type | Threat |
|---|---|---|---|
| [The Wound](the-wound.md) | Central Europe | Reality collapse | Extreme |
| [The Sunken Halls](the-sunken-halls.md) | Mediterranean | Spatial distortion | Severe |
| [The Bleeding Forest](the-bleeding-forest.md) | West Africa | Biological | Severe |
| [The Scar of Voices](the-scar-of-voices.md) | Middle East | Auditory | Moderate |
| [The Mirror Coast](the-mirror-coast.md) | East Africa | Reflection | Moderate |
| [The Burning Calm](the-burning-calm.md) | Arabia | Temperature | Severe |
| [The Stone Garden](the-stone-garden.md) | India/SE Asia | Petrification | Moderate |
| [The Pale Maze](the-pale-maze.md) | China/Korea | Mixed | Extreme |
| [Northern Wastes](northern-wastes.md) | Former Siberia | Total corruption | Impassable |
| [Antarctic Chaos](antarctic-chaos.md) | Antarctica | Temporal | Impassable |
| [The Silence](../../07-geography/the-silence.md) | S. South America | **Natural deadzone** | Special |

Two more appear only in the Confederacy chapter (§24) and are absent from the master
table — likely smaller:

| Zone | Location | Type |
|---|---|---|
| [The Drowning Fields](the-drowning-fields.md) | Confederacy | Impossible water behavior |
| [The Temporal Reefs](the-temporal-reefs.md) | Confederacy waters | Ships emerge before they left |

*The Silence is technically not a glitch zone — the Domain is absent there, not
corrupted. It lives under [Geography](../../07-geography/the-silence.md).*
