# The Sunken Halls

**Location:** Mediterranean · **Type:** Spatial distortion · **Threat:** Severe

Spatially twisted ruins beneath the partially drained Mediterranean — inside
[Imperial](../../07-geography/feudal-empire/_index.md) territory, near the
resource-extraction economy of Lowgate.

## Open threads

- Ruins of *what*? Pre-collapse cities drowned, then exposed, then twisted — prime hunting ground for [relic traffickers](../../09-power-and-conflict/criminal-underworld.md).
- Spatial distortion + ruins = rooms that shouldn't connect do. Maps are lies here; guides are priceless.
- † [Brakka](../../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/brakka.md), god of the drained sea, feels a *tide* pulling toward the Halls on certain nights — and suspects the Mediterranean was folded, not destroyed. If he's right, the Halls are a seam, not a ruin.
