# Glitch Phenomena Catalogue

Beyond creature manifestations, [glitch zones](glitch-zones/_index.md) create
environmental phenomena that warp reality.

## Spatial phenomena

- **Folded Space** — distance becomes inconsistent.
- **Recursive Geography** — locations connecting to themselves impossibly.
- **Compression Zones** — space compressed. Entry usually fatal.
- **The Thinning** — the barrier between reality and *something else* becomes permeable.

## Temporal phenomena

- **Time Pockets** — localized areas where time moves differently.
- **Stutters** — moments that repeat.
- **Cascade Failures** — effects happen before causes.

## Physical phenomena

- **Gravity Inversions/Fluctuations** — localized changes in gravitational direction or strength.
- **Matter State Shifts** — solid becomes liquid without temperature change.
- **Momentum Theft** — kinetic energy vanishes. Hearts stop beating.
