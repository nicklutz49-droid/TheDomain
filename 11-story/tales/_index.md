## Conventions for new tales

- One file per story; frontmatter `type: story`, plus `secrecy` for what the tale *reveals* (a story can show a reader restricted truth its narrator doesn't understand).
- Anchor every tale to at least one entity file and link both ways when the tale establishes new facts about it.
- Tales are texture, not necessarily truth — an unreliable narrator doesn't change canon. If a tale *does* establish canon, log it in [continuity notes](../../00-meta/continuity-notes.md).
