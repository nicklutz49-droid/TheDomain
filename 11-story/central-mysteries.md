# Central Mysteries

The setting's load-bearing questions. Answering any of them on the page is a
campaign- or series-level event.

1. **What does [the Entity](../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md) ultimately want? Can its preparations succeed?**
2. **What price will humanity pay for survival?**
3. **What happened in [the Pale](../07-geography/pale-sovereignty.md)? What has [Palladia](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/palladia.md) become?**
4. **Can the [Cabal](../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-cabal.md) achieve ascension?**
5. **What do [the Ascended](../04-divine-beings/ascended/_index.md) know?**

## Mysteries implied but unlisted in the source

- Who or what is [Whisper](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/whisper.md)?
- How do the [Silence Prophecies](../10-supplementary/prophecies-and-sacred-texts.md) predate the Domain?
- Who was [the Unnamed](../04-divine-beings/sealed-gods/the-unnamed.md), and why was the erasure total?
- What waits at the frozen heart of [Antarctic Chaos](../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/antarctic-chaos.md)?
