# Narrative Seeds

Canon hooks, ready to run. Each links to its load-bearing entities.

1. **A pilgrim discovers evidence changing everything they know about the gods.** → [Pilgrimage](../10-supplementary/pilgrimage.md), [Religion](../08-civilization/religion.md), heresy stakes.
2. **A Fluxborn begins having dreams that don't match the Entity's usual patterns.** → [Fluxborn](../05-empowered-humans/fluxborn.md), [the Entity](../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md). (Whose dreams are they, then?)
3. **Someone escapes the Pale with knowledge of what Palladia is planning.** → [Pale Sovereignty](../07-geography/pale-sovereignty.md), [the Pale Maze](../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/the-pale-maze.md).
4. **A minor god discovers the truth** and must decide: accept sealing, try to hide, or attempt something unprecedented. → [Minor gods](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/_index.md), [Sealed gods](../04-divine-beings/sealed-gods/_index.md).
5. **The Twelve notice patterns suggesting the Entity is accelerating preparations.** → [The Twelve](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/_index.md), [Lunar Complex](../03-hidden-truth/lunar-complex.md).
6. **A Hollow manifestation displays behavior suggesting communication.** → [Hollow](../06-threats-and-anomalies/hollow/_index.md), [Ithrazel](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/ithrazel.md), [Hollow Whisperers](../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-hollow-whisperers.md).
7. **An Architect team repairs a relay and accidentally activates a message from before the collapse.** → [Architects](../05-empowered-humans/architects.md), [infrastructure](../02-the-domain/infrastructure.md), [ARIA](../03-hidden-truth/aria.md).
