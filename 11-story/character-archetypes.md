# Character Archetypes

Protagonist shapes native to this setting — each built to collide with a hidden
truth.

- **The Questioning God** — a minor deity who suspects the truth. *(Prototypes: [Seraphex](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/seraphex.md), [Axiom](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/axiom.md).)*
- **The Gifted Pawn** — a [Fluxborn](../05-empowered-humans/fluxborn.md) wrestling with knowledge that their powers serve someone else's agenda.
- **The True Believer** — someone devoted to their god who discovers evidence challenging their faith.
- **The Hollow Hunter** — a professional who has seen too much. *(Employers: [glitch specialists](../05-empowered-humans/architects.md), [the Pilgrims](../09-power-and-conflict/mercenary-companies.md).)*
- **The Cabal Idealist** — someone discovering how far the organization will go. *(Endpoint: [the Crane's Shadow](../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-cranes-shadow.md).)*
- **The Architect Heretic** — a scholar whose research has led to dangerous conclusions. *(Patron saint: [Callum Vance](../05-empowered-humans/legendary-figures/callum-vance.md).)*
- **The Entity's Focus** — someone who has drawn [the Entity's](../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md) particular attention — but doesn't know why.
