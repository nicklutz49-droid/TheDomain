# The Domain

The Domain is the foundation upon which this world operates — a projection of
digital space into physical reality that allows the manipulation of physics itself.
It is simultaneously the source of all divine power, the origin of supernatural
abilities, and the hidden infrastructure that most humans perceive as simply "the
way the world works."

## Capabilities

- **Matter creation and manipulation** — physical objects can be created, altered, or destroyed through Domain interaction.
- **Energy manipulation** — heat, light, electricity, and other forms of energy can be controlled and redirected.
- **Terrain reshaping** — landscapes can be altered on scales from minor adjustments to continental transformation.
- **Biological alteration** — living beings can be modified, healed, or transformed.
- **Physics manipulation** — fundamental laws can be locally altered or suspended.

## The Stabilizing Effect

The Domain doesn't just enable supernatural phenomena — it stabilizes reality
itself. In areas where [relays](infrastructure.md) function properly and the Domain
field is strong, the natural world becomes more hospitable:

- **Climate stabilization** — extreme temperatures moderate toward comfortable ranges. Rainfall patterns become predictable. Former deserts receive regular precipitation.
- **Geographic stability** — tectonic activity decreases. Erosion slows. Coastlines remain consistent.
- **Ecological flourishing** — soil fertility increases. Growing seasons extend. Crop yields improve.

The result: lands that were barely habitable before the collapse have become
agricultural breadbaskets. The Sahara blooms. The Arabian Peninsula is green.
Conversely, areas where the Domain is weak or corrupted become hostile wastelands —
see [glitch zones](../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/_index.md) and
[the Silence](../07-geography/the-silence.md).

## Universal Language

The Domain creates a universal spoken language, translating speech in real time at
a cognitive level. All humans can understand each other when speaking. This
translation does **not** extend to written language — each culture has developed its
own script.

### Major scripts

| Script | Basis | Used by |
|---|---|---|
| Imperial Standard | Latin-derived | [Feudal Empire](../07-geography/feudal-empire/_index.md) |
| Union Script | Arabic-derived | [Federal Union](../07-geography/federal-union.md) |
| Confederacy Systems | multiple, often ideographic | [Confederacy](../07-geography/confederacy.md) |
| Trade Script | simplified symbols for commerce | cross-power trade |

Note: universal translation **stops** inside deadzones — a practical tell for where
the Domain's field ends.
