# Flux

Flux is the term humans use for the energy that flows through the
[Domain](the-domain.md) — the raw power that enables all supernatural phenomena. It
can be sensed, channeled, and manipulated by those with the knowledge or innate
ability to do so.

## What it actually is (restricted)

Flux isn't mystical energy in the traditional sense — it's the active processing
power of the Domain made tangible. When an
[Architect](../05-empowered-humans/architects.md) shapes Flux, they're essentially
making requests of the Domain's systems. When a
[Fluxborn](../05-empowered-humans/fluxborn.md) channels their abilities, they're
accessing permissions granted by [the Entity](../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md).

## Everyday presence

- Stored in synthetic crystals as [Flux Shards](../08-civilization/economics.md), the world's universal hard currency.
- Channeled through [Flux Circuitry](../05-empowered-humans/architects.md) in devices from holo-comms to healing matrices.
- Souls are widely believed to *be* Flux — see [Death & Afterlife](../08-civilization/death-and-afterlife.md).
- Nature has adapted to its ambient presence — see [Ecology](../10-supplementary/ecology.md).
