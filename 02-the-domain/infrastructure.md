# Domain Infrastructure

## The Moon Core

The central processor of the Domain resides in an underground complex on the moon,
hidden from observation. The core projects the Domain's field through a network of
satellites and surface relays, maintaining coverage across the entire Earth–Moon
system. The Domain's effective range extends approximately **10 light-seconds** from
the core.

*(GM-only: the moon holds far more than a server farm — see
[The Lunar Complex](../03-hidden-truth/lunar-complex.md).)*

## Satellite Relays

Orbital satellites distribute the Domain's field across Earth's surface, ensuring
relatively consistent coverage. These satellites prevent the gods from becoming too
weak when the moon is below the horizon, though divine power still waxes and wanes
somewhat with lunar visibility.

## Surface Relays

Underground nodes scattered across the planet serve as local amplifiers and
stabilizers. Humans have built temples around these relays, treating them as sacred
sites without understanding their true function. See
[Religion](../08-civilization/religion.md) and
[Pilgrimage](../10-supplementary/pilgrimage.md).

## System Self-Defense

The Domain actively prevents any attempt to reach the moon core through:

1. Subtle interference,
2. Warnings via nightmares,
3. In extreme cases, intentional [Hollow](../06-threats-and-anomalies/hollow/_index.md) attacks.

The technology for space travel has been lost, and the Domain ensures it stays that
way. (The [Lunar Observers](../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-lunar-observers.md)
have identified the moon core's existence anyway.)
