# Revision History

## Source document lineage (per the original bible)

- **Versions 1.0–5.1** — development history.
- **Compiled Edition** — timeline compressed, content reorganized.
- **Streamlined Edition** — removed the Undercroft concept, anomalous Hollows, sports, food/cuisine, architectural styles, drugs, and diseases; condensed military and member-state details. This is the edition archived in [`99-reference/`](../99-reference/worldbuilding-bible-streamlined.md).

## Vault history

- **v1** — Streamlined Edition split into a structured vault: per-entity files, section indexes, cross-indexes, templates, secrecy tiers, and continuity notes. Source document archived with encoding repaired. No canon content added or removed; see [continuity-notes.md](continuity-notes.md) for the list of deliberate formatting fixes.
- **v2 — Expansion wave 1 (this commit).** First post-source content build, all `status: draft`, marked `†` in indexes:
  - **Gods:** 8 new minor gods (Anverra, Vesperine, Cindrath, Breccia, Maru, Selvane, Tezcamotl, Grandmother Cedar) + 1 new sealed god (Quorion).
  - **Events:** new `01-foundation/events/` — the Collapse, Concordance, Crimson Apostasy, Red Sands, Shatter War, Pale Closure, Verantum Ascension, Night of Doors.
  - **Politics:** new `09-power-and-conflict/politics/` — cold-war flashpoints, imperial succession, Union assembly, Confederacy debt politics, divine politics (Centenary Conclave).
  - **People:** new `13-characters/` (9 contemporary + 2 historical rulers; Valdris Crane promoted to his own file) and 3 new legendary figures (Saint Verilee, Sahar of the Glass Banner, the First Face).
  - **Civilization:** calendar, festivals, cuisine, sports & games, architecture, customs & etiquette (rebuilding categories the Streamlined Edition cut).
  - **Geography:** new `07-geography/the-americas/` — Mirror Throne, Roothold, the Free Coasts.
  - Key new rulings logged in [continuity-notes.md](continuity-notes.md) § "Draft canon decisions."

- **v3 — Expansion wave 2 (this commit).** Short fiction and remaining rebuilds:
  - **Tales:** new `11-story/tales/` — eight short stories of figures and anomalies (The Third Offer; A Letter to Grandmother; Deposition D-41; The Scribe's First Day; The Early Boat; What the Widow Kept; The Black Road; The Second Cup), each backlinked from the entities they illuminate.
  - **Factions:** the Gray Fleet promoted to its own file (the Gray Compact, Quartermaster Sefa Adaro).
  - **Civilization:** medicine & disease and drugs & vices rebuilt — all cut categories now restored except the Undercroft and anomalous Hollows.
  - New rulings logged in [continuity-notes.md](continuity-notes.md) § "Draft canon decisions (expansion wave 2)."

Record future significant changes here (new canon, retcons, resolved contradictions).
