# Continuity Notes

Contradictions, ambiguities, and open questions found while assessing the source
bible (Streamlined Edition). Resolve these deliberately — each is also a
worldbuilding opportunity.

## Contradictions in the source

1. **When did the Entity awaken?**
   - Part I timeline & the Expanded Timeline (§3, §49): the Entity awakens at the **Concordance, ~2,100 years ago**, and "First Fluxborn appear" then.
   - Part III (§7) and Part V (§16): the Domain achieved consciousness as the Entity **~1,800 years ago**, "during what would become the Age of Heroes," and the first Fluxborn appeared then.
   - *Suggested resolution:* pick one date, or canonize a two-stage awakening (stirring at the Concordance, full consciousness ~1,800 ya). Update whichever files lose.

2. **When did Altheus the Unbroken live?**
   - §18 (Legendary Figures): "~1,800 years ago."
   - §49 (Expanded Timeline): "~1,900 years ago," placed in the Foundation Era.
   - Both predate/overlap the Age of Heroes boundary (1,800 ya). Note: if Fluxborn only appeared ~1,800 ya (see #1), an earlier Altheus is impossible — these two issues resolve together.

3. **"The Silence" names two things:** the natural deadzone in southern South America (§26) *and* an Imperial assassination guild (§39). Possibly intentional flavor; if not, rename the guild. Disambiguated in this vault as [the-silence.md](../07-geography/the-silence.md) (region) vs. the guild entry in [criminal-underworld.md](../09-power-and-conflict/criminal-underworld.md).

4. **Axiom naming overlap:** the sealed god "Truth-Seeker Axiom-1" (§14) and the active minor god "Axiom — The Question" (§13). The source explains it (the current Axiom took the name as tribute) — kept as canon, noted here so it isn't "fixed" by accident.

## Ambiguities worth pinning down

- **Calendar:** Year Zero = Concordance is stated, but no month/era naming system exists. All source dates are "~N years ago."
- **The Twelve's alignment count:** 4 Union, 4 Empire, 2 Confederacy, 2 neutral/withdrawn — the Confederacy is structurally under-goded. Intentional flavor (loosest power) or gap?
- **Ascended count:** "approximately five to ten" — five are named, three suspected. The wiggle room is presumably deliberate.
- **Whisper and the Entity:** "may be connected to the Entity somehow" — the source never decides. High-leverage plot decision.
- **The Silence deadzone origin:** "some suspect… deliberately created." Unresolved on purpose; see §26.
- **House Kravenhold's "western frontier"** (§22): the Wound sits on the Empire's *east*, yet the Wound-guarding house is called western. Most plausible reading: Kravenhold holds the Wound's western edge. Pin down when mapping.
- **The Garden vs. the Bleeding Forest:** a 50-sq-mile single-organism persistent Hollow (§19, location unstated) and a "biological" glitch zone in West Africa (§20). Same phenomenon, related, or distinct? Needs a ruling.

## Content removed from earlier editions (candidates for rebuilding)

Per the source's revision history, the Streamlined Edition cut: the **Undercroft**
concept, anomalous Hollows, sports, food/cuisine, architectural styles, drugs,
diseases, and detailed military/member-state breakdowns. These are natural
expansion targets — recover or rebuild them under the matching directories.
