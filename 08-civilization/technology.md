# Technology and Infrastructure

All advanced technology runs on [Flux Circuitry](../05-empowered-humans/architects.md)
channeling the [Domain](../02-the-domain/the-domain.md). See also
[Daily Life](daily-life.md).

## Available technology

| Category | Technologies |
|---|---|
| Communication | Holo-comms, voice boxes, message stones |
| Transportation | Airships, Flux-powered trains, sailing ships |
| Computing | Analog computers, mechanical calculators (**no digital**) |
| Medicine | Healing matrices, diagnostic arrays, prosthetics |
| Weapons | Flux blades, projection weapons, siege engines |
| Defense | Reality locks, containment devices, personal shields |

## Lost technology

- **Space travel** — actively prevented by the Domain (see [system self-defense](../02-the-domain/infrastructure.md))
- Digital computing
- Automobiles and aircraft
- Nuclear technology
- Advanced chemistry

Exception: [the Silence](../07-geography/the-silence.md) develops purely mechanical
technology — steam, clockwork, chemistry — because Flux devices don't work there.
