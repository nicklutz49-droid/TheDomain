# Economics and Currency

## The Flux Shard

Universal hard currency: small synthetic crystals storing measurable
[Flux](../02-the-domain/flux.md) energy.

| Shard | Tier | Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| Blue | Low | powers a household device for a year |
| Red | Medium | cost of airship passage |
| Black | High | state-level trade |

## Major currencies

- **Empire — Imperial Mark:** physical coin with Flux-trace. Value backed by the Emperor's guarantee.
- **Union — Merit Credit:** digital tally tied to a citizen's contribution.
- **Confederacy — Honor Bond:** debt-and-favors system. Wealth is measured by who owes you.

## Cross-power commerce

The [Silk Passage](../07-geography/neutral-territories.md) connects all three
economies; [Trade Script](../02-the-domain/the-domain.md) exists for exactly this.
[Syreth](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/syreth.md) — The Gilded Ledger — is the
divine hand on the scales (and is quietly buying influence across all three powers).
