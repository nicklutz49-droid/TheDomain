# Death, Burial, and Afterlife

## Dominant beliefs

- **Return Theory** — the soul is [Flux](../02-the-domain/flux.md); it disperses back into the Domain. Most common.
- **Divine Collection** — gods collect devoted worshippers' souls.
- **Void Theory** — death is the end. Privately common.
- **Hollow Theory (forbidden)** — souls that don't disperse become [Hollow](../06-threats-and-anomalies/hollow/_index.md).

## Burial practices

- **Empire** — cremation. Ashes in ancestral vaults.
- **Union** — sky burial. Bodies consumed by [Cloud-Rays](../10-supplementary/ecology.md).
- **Confederacy** — sea burial. Bodies committed to [Naveris](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/naveris.md).

## Rituals

- **The Vigil** — someone watches the body ~72 hours as Flux disperses.
- **The Speaking** — sharing memories during the Vigil.
- **The Binding Release** — oaths dissolved before death.
