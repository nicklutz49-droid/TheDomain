# Art, Music, and Performance

## Visual arts

- **Flux-Painting** — light manipulation. Static paintings with impossible luminosity; dynamic paintings that change over time.
- **Living Steel Work** — sculptures that slowly shift position over years. (Raw material: [Iron-Vines](../10-supplementary/ecology.md).)
- **Light Sculpture** — geometric forms of pure structured light.

## Music

- **Conventional instruments** — natural sounds.
- **Resonance instruments** — Flux-circuitry amplifying and modifying sound.
- **Forbidden music** — certain combinations create dangerous Domain resonance.

## Theater

- **Imperial Drama** — formal, structured, clear moral messages.
- **Union Documentary Theater** — recreating historical events accurately.
- **Confederacy Identity Theater** — performers literally *become* characters through mask-magic (see the [Mask-Weavers](../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-mask-weavers.md)).
