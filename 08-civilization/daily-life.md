# Daily Life and Modern Amenities

For most people in civilized areas, daily life feels contemporary.

## What people take for granted

- **Communication devices** — handheld holo-projectors, essentially phones. Project holographic displays, allow voice and visual communication, access information networks, handle transactions.
- **Climate control** — buildings maintain comfortable temperatures year-round.
- **Lighting** — ambient lighting responds to presence and preference.
- **Running water** — hot and cold, on demand. Sewage systems.
- **Food preservation** — household stasis units keep food fresh indefinitely.
- **Entertainment** — holo-projectors display recorded performances, news, sporting events.

## Transportation

- **No personal vehicles** — airship technology and trains replaced the need.
- **Public transit** — tram systems, elevated rail, water transit in coastal cities. Cities designed for walking.
- **Airships** — primary distance transportation: commercial passenger routes, private craft, urban air-taxis, small village airships for frontier communities.
- **Trains** — high-speed Flux-trains for intercity travel; freight rail for heavy goods.
- **Animals** — horses in rural areas; wagons for local goods transport.
