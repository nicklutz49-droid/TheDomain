# Marriage, Family, and Kinship

## Empire

- **Noble marriage** — political contracts. Dowry, succession, adultery penalties negotiated.
- **Common marriage** — contractual. A **Year of Proving** before formalizing.
- Extended families share housing.

## Union

- **Partnership model** — any configuration of consenting adults. Polyamory accepted; 15% of households include three or more partners.
- **Child allocation** — children considered community assets. The state can intervene.

## Confederacy

- **Debt-marriage** — creates debt relationships between families.
- Children inherit debt status. At 16, they begin independent debt-tracking.

## Open threads

- Union "child allocation" is quietly the most dystopian line in the civilization chapters — how often does the state intervene, and who decides?
- Cross-power marriages: an Imperial contract, a Union partnership, and a Confederacy debt can't describe the same union. Border families must improvise legal chimeras.
