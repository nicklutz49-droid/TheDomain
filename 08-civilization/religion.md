# Religion and Worship

The gods are **real**. Faith isn't about believing in the unseen — it's about
navigating relationships with powerful, present, inconsistent beings.

## Types of worship

- **Transactional** — offer tribute, expect return. Most common.
- **Devotional** — life dedication to a single god. May receive visions, enhanced abilities.
- **Philosophical** — treating gods as forces of nature to study and predict.

## Temple practices

Temples are built around [surface relays](../02-the-domain/infrastructure.md) —
sacred sites whose true function no worshipper understands.

- **Empire** — grand structures emphasizing hierarchy. Progressively sacred spaces.
- **Union** — community centers. Services include discussion and collective decision-making.
- **Confederacy** — performative spaces. Mask ceremonies and debt ledgers.

## Heresy

Any claim that gods are artificial or technological is **absolutely forbidden**.
Violators are *unmade* — existence erased.
