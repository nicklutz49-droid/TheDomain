# Drugs and Vices

Rebuilding a category the Streamlined Edition cut. Canon kept the Smokehouse (the
Empire's drug guild); this file supplies its inventory.

## The common vices

Ale, wine, rice-spirits, smoke-leaf: universal, legal, taxed. The three powers
moralize differently — the Empire taxes vice (waste made useful), the Union
schedules it (transparency extends to your habits), the Confederacy runs it on
debt (the house always collects, literally).

## Domain-age drugs

- **Glowdust** — powdered lumen-tree bark. Mild euphoria; the user's skin faintly luminesces for hours. Festival-legal in the Confederacy, scheduled in the Union, an Imperial court fashion officially deplored and universally available. The [Smokehouse's](../09-power-and-conflict/criminal-underworld.md) volume product.
- **Still-water** — a suppressant brewed (illegally everywhere) from ghost-grass. Damps the user's presence in the Domain: Fluxborn report their gifts going quiet; the hunted swear it baffles divine notice. The [Freedom Network](../09-power-and-conflict/criminal-underworld.md) moves more of it than any pleasure-dealer. Chronic use: the user starts *staying* dim.
- **Echo-resin** — the grief drug. Distilled near glitch zones; a dose replays a chosen memory with full sensory weight. Medically used (once, supervised) for the Speaking when a death was sudden; abused, it is the most addictive substance known, for the obvious reason: the memory never degrades, and the present does. Smokehouse premium stock; the widow-trade, dealers call it, and even they say it with a wince.
- **Spark** — Flux-shard shavings dissolved in spirit. Burst of energy, faint electrical arcing at the fingertips, cardiac roulette. The dock-worker's dram; kills a few every Still Days when the field runs thin and the dose doesn't.
- **Mirror-smoke** — new, expensive, whispered to be cut with powdered obsidian from [Mirror Throne](../07-geography/the-americas/mirror-throne.md) shard-glass. Users report being *watched*, and describe the sensation as the point. The clinics report something worse: a few users' reflections now lag.

## Forbidden absolutely

- **Hollow derivatives** — anything rendered from Hollow remains. Every power's law agrees for once: possession is a talking-to-the-Wound offense. The [Crane's Shadow](../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-cranes-shadow.md) is rumored to pay for stock anyway.
- **God-ash** — allegedly scraped from sites of divine manifestation; allegedly does nothing; allegedly. The trade in it is real even if the substance isn't, which tells you something about worship.
