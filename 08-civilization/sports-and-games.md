# Sports and Games

Rebuilding a category the Streamlined Edition cut. Canon already had crowds
watching "sporting events" on holo-projectors — this is what they're watching.

## The big spectator sports

- **Regatta** (Empire and everywhere airships fly) — house-liveried airship racing through pylon courses; the Imperial Grand Regatta at Thessalar is the year's biggest crowd. Great Houses treat podium order as foreign policy.
- **The Gauntlet** (Union) — the national obsession: six-person teams through a course mixing athletics, engineering problems, and logic gates, redesigned every year and kept secret until the starting bell. Explicitly built as "the Exam, but fun"; Gauntlet stars outshine actors.
- **Hundred-Hands** (Confederacy) — harbor regatta rowed in hundred-oar longboats crewed by rival debt-houses; the race is the negotiation. Followed by **Identity duels**: competitive mask-theater where two performers play *each other* until the crowd can't tell — the judged categories include "mercy."

## Played everywhere

- **Circuit** — the cross-power board game: tile-laying with Flux-circuit patterns where completed circuits capture territory. Folk version in every tavern; the formal game has a ranking ladder, famous openings, and a persistent rumor that master-level play *teaches something* — the [Architects'](../05-empowered-humans/architects.md) academies quietly recruit from the ladder.
- **Stone-counting** (children, universal) — the pilgrim game: cairn-building against the clock, from [Breccia's](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/breccia.md) wayside customs.
- **Spire-diving** (urban youth, semi-legal) — climbing city architecture and diving into water or nets. Every generation of parents declares it new and fatal; it is neither.

## The dark side

- **Hollow-baiting** — illegal everywhere: staged bouts against captured Class I Hollows. The [Shadow Guilds](../09-power-and-conflict/criminal-underworld.md) run pits in three Imperial cities. Occasionally a pit's stock is misclassified, and then a district burns.
- **Accord-breaking pools** — gambling on which power next violates the [Fluxborn Accords](../01-foundation/events/battle-of-red-sands.md). Tasteless, thriving.

## Open threads

- A Gauntlet course designer who's secretly a Cabal recruiter; a Circuit prodigy whose games reproduce god-script fragments — sport as the setting's most innocent-looking doorway into its secrets.
