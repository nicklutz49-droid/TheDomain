# Education Systems

## Empire

- **Tutor system** for the wealthy.
- **Guild apprenticeship** for trades — 7–12 years.
- **Imperial War College** for officers.

## Union

- **Universal basic education**, ages 6–16.
- **The Great Exam** at 16 determines career placement.
- **Specialized academies** afterward.
- **Architect training:** 10–15 years, **40% graduation rate** (see [Architects](../05-empowered-humans/architects.md)).

## Confederacy

- **Family-based learning.**
- **Apprentice markets.**
- **Mask schools** for social education (see [Confederacy culture](../07-geography/confederacy.md)).

- What happens to the 60% who wash out of Architect training? A large population of half-trained Domain manipulators is a security problem and a story mine.
