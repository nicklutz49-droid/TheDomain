# Festivals and Holidays

The ritual year, built on the [calendar](calendar.md).

## Universal

- **Concordance Day** (New Year, first moonrise after the Still Days) — bells at moonrise, oaths renewed, the year's first contract signed at dawn for luck. In [Crossroads](../07-geography/neutral-territories.md), thirteen lamps are lit at the old summit ground; the thirteenth is lit last and no one will say why.
- **The Still Days** (year's end) — vigils, lamps, no contracts; the Confederacy's Bare Days. See [calendar](calendar.md).
- **Accord Walk** (~Syrethis) — descendants of Red Sands walk the Glass Flats together; the one cross-power rite ([Battle of Red Sands](../01-foundation/events/battle-of-red-sands.md)).

## Empire

- **Feast of Chains** (Verantine) — the Chain of Service celebrated link by link: households host their *own* liege and servants at one table, the only night rank sits mixed. Oath renewals; enormous food; zero waste, by law and pride. *(The Crimson Apostasy happened at a Feast of Chains — in Thessalar, a lamp is left unlit for the god no one names.)*
- **The Wound Vigil** (Ithrazen) — frontier remembrance for those the Wound has taken; watch-fires along the Kravenhold line answer each other at dusk.
- **Museum Night** (Thessalyn) — Thessalar opens its halls free; the line to see [Iron Hand Marcus](../05-empowered-humans/legendary-figures/iron-hand-marcus.md) runs all night, and veterans touch the barrier's base for luck, which the curators have stopped fighting.

## Union

- **Examen Eve** (night before the Great Exam, Fourth Month) — a whole society goes quiet so sixteen-year-olds can sleep. Lights dim by ordinance; strangers leave sweets on doorsteps of exam households.
- **The Lantern Audit** (Twelfth Month) — the year's failures written on paper lanterns and burned publicly; officials burn theirs *first*, by law. The Transparency Crisis has made this year's Audit politically radioactive ([Union Assembly](../09-power-and-conflict/politics/union-assembly.md)).
- **Ascension Day** (provincial, ~Omnethi) — honors the [Ascended](../04-divine-beings/ascended/_index.md); in three provinces it specifically honors the [Verantum Ascension](../01-foundation/events/the-verantum-ascension.md), which is awkward, because it celebrates a sealing.

## Confederacy

- **Ledger's Turning** (Full Ledger month) — debts recited publicly; each house forgives *one*, by choice, with theater. Reputations are made by which debt you forgive.
- **Storm-Rest** (typhoon season's end) — [Korrath](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/korrath.md) placated with drum-fleets and kite-offerings; [Maru](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/maru.md) thanked for every hull that came home.
- **The Launching** (per port, spring tides) — new ships named and first-wakes cut; a Maru coin under every mast-step.

## Dark observances

- **D-41 Night** (~12 years running, unofficial) — door-walkers and the bereaved of the [Night of Doors](../01-foundation/events/the-night-of-doors.md) leave doors ajar with a lamp behind them. Authorities discourage it. It spreads anyway.
