# The Silence

**Location:** Southern South America (former Patagonia)

A **natural deadzone** where the [Domain's](../02-the-domain/the-domain.md) field
doesn't reach. Population approximately 200,000.

## What the absence means

- No divine influence — gods cannot manifest.
- [Fluxborn](../05-empowered-humans/fluxborn.md) powers fail.
- [Universal translation](../02-the-domain/the-domain.md) stops.

## The people

Fiercely independent, viewing gods as dangerous entities. Have developed purely
**mechanical technology** — steam, clockwork, chemical processes — the only place on
Earth advancing down the pre-collapse path.

## The secret

Some suspect the Domain's absence isn't natural — that pre-collapse humans
**deliberately created** the deadzone. (Unresolved in canon; the
[Silence Guardians](../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-silence-guardians.md)
protect the region, and the [Silence Prophecies](../10-supplementary/prophecies-and-sacred-texts.md)
— which predate the Domain — originate here.)
