# Neutral Territories

## The Silk Passage

Trade route connecting all three powers through Central Asia, threading around
glitch zones. Major cities: **Crossroads**, **Haven's Rest**, **Fort Perseverance**.

- Commerce: the natural home of [Trade Script](../02-the-domain/the-domain.md) and cross-power exchange — see [Economics](../08-civilization/economics.md).
- Crime: the [Silk Passage Syndicate](../09-power-and-conflict/criminal-underworld.md) runs smuggling, protection, and information brokerage along it.

## The Americas

Western continents host myriad unaligned nations — many **directly ruled by gods the
Twelve haven't intervened with**. North America was reshaped with a massive inland
sea. Hundreds of nations, city-states, and tribal territories.

*(This is the largest under-written region in canon: god-kings the Concordance
doesn't police. Enormous expansion room.)*

## Impassable regions

- **Northern Asia** — completely consumed by glitch activity: the [Northern Wastes](../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/northern-wastes.md).
- **Antarctica** — temporal corruption; at the heart, something has stopped time entirely: [Antarctic Chaos](../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/antarctic-chaos.md).
