# The Undercroft

*Reconstructing the concept the Streamlined Edition cut.* The Undercroft is the
world beneath the world: the buried strata of pre-collapse civilization that
every major city is built on top of. Basements under basements, drowned transit
galleries, service tunnels that outlived their services — reshaped by the
Collapse, colonized by three thousand years of cellars, and never, anywhere,
fully mapped.

## The rule of depth

Delvers reckon it simply: **three floors down is yesterday; thirty is before the
gods.** The upper Undercroft is mundane — storage, mushroom farms, smugglers'
ways, the cool vaults where Imperial families age their
[cheeses beside their ancestors](../08-civilization/cuisine.md). The middle
depths are the relic country that feeds the
[traffickers' trade](../09-power-and-conflict/criminal-underworld.md). The deep
levels are pre-collapse in situ: intact rooms, unreadable signage, machinery that
is not always off. The deepest doors bear rows of sacred symbols —
[god-script](../02-the-domain/god-script.md) — and some of them are **warm**.

**Never open a warm door.** It is the Undercroft's one universal law, older than
any guild, and it is [Vantus's](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/vantus.md) only
commandment. Every delver knows a story about a crew that did. No two stories
end the same way, which is worse than if they did.

## Who goes down

- **The Delvers' Guild** — chartered in most great cities: licensed entries, depth-bonds, the map-tithe (every expedition files its survey; the Guild's composite maps are its treasure and its power).
- **Relic crews** — unlicensed, faster, shorter-lived.
- **The dispossessed** — the Undercroft's permanent population: [outcasts](../10-supplementary/outcasts.md), debt-runners, the Freedom Network's safe-cellars.
- **Glitch Specialists** — because errors pool downward. Gnawer nests, glitch pockets, bad air that isn't air.

## Known deep sites

- **Thessalar's temple undercroft** — the most famous door in the world: [Malevictus](../04-divine-beings/sealed-gods/malevictus.md) sleeps behind it.
- **The Sunken Halls** — the Undercroft's drowned Mediterranean wing, spatially twisted ([zone file](../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/the-sunken-halls.md)).
- **The Verantum Stacks** — sealed archive levels beneath the Meridian Archives, predating the Archives; [Velia Sorn](../13-characters/velia-sorn.md) has clearance for the top two and has begun wondering what the ladder past them is for.
- **Buried doors at the ends of [Breccia's](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/breccia.md) black roads** — see [The Black Road](../11-story/tales/the-black-road.md).

## The deep question (GM-only)

The [Collapse file](../01-foundation/events/the-collapse.md) leaves one thread
hanging: *where was the Domain's ground-side command center?* The Undercroft is
where that thread goes. Somewhere under one of the great cities is the room ARIA
was administered from — and the Entity knows exactly which warm door it's behind.
