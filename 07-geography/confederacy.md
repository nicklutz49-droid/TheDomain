# The Confederacy

**Core territories:** Eastern and Central India, Southeast Asia, Madagascar, Japan,
Pacific Islands, Australia

## Government

The loosest major power — an alliance rather than a state. The **debt system** binds
society together.

## Geography

- **Indian Territories** — Ganges agricultural powerhouse. **Freeport Yrshen** (pop. 1,500,000, named for [Yrshen](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/yrshen.md)) is the de facto capital. **Resonance City** houses the major [Architect](../05-empowered-humans/architects.md) training center.
- **Southeast Asia** — the Peninsula Alliance, **Spice Gate** city-state (pop. 1,000,000), the Thousand Islands.
- **Island Nations** — Madagascar, the Japanese islands (**Naveris' Rest**, pop. 700,000, named for [Naveris](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/naveris.md)), Australia.

## Culture

Performance, honor, intricate social dancing. Citizens wear **masks** indicating
"social face" (see the [Mask-Weavers](../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-mask-weavers.md)).
Children are born with **Life Debt** to their parents. Social taboo: **breaking
debts**.

## Patron gods

[Yrshen](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/yrshen.md) and
[Naveris](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/naveris.md); the minor god
[Korrath](../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/korrath.md) rules a mountainous region.
