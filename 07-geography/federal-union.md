# The Federal Union

**Core territories:** Central and Eastern Africa, Middle East, Southern Persia,
Western India

## Government

A federation of over forty nations bound by shared institutions. Bureaucratic,
meritocratic, data-driven. The **Great Exam** at age 16 determines career placement
(see [Education](../08-civilization/education.md)).

## Geography

- **African Territories** — the Green Heart agricultural powerhouse. Eastern coast separated by a thin sea.
- **Middle Eastern Territories** — Union heartland. Densely populated. **Verantum** (pop. 4,000,000) is the capital — named for [Veranthos](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/veranthos.md).
- **Major cities** — **Kythera's Reach** (pop. 1,200,000, named for [Kythera](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/kythera.md)), **Persian Gate**, **Western Shore**.

## Culture

Hyper-intellectualism and merit. Minimalist fashion with glowing **Status Strips**.
Social taboo: **secrecy**.
