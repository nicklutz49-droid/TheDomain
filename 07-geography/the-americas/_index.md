# The Americas

The western continents: hundreds of nations, city-states, and tribal territories,
**many directly ruled by gods the Twelve have never intervened with** (canon).
North America is reshaped around a massive **Inland Sea**. No Concordance, no
Accords, no cold war — a different answer to the same divine question.

## The Far Accord (rumor)

Why have the Twelve never intervened? The persistent rumor — deflected identically
by every god asked — is the **Far Accord**: during the Divine Chaos, the fragments
that fled west refused the future Concordance but swore *mutual ignorance* — you do
not look at us, we do not look at you. If true, it predates Year Zero and is the
oldest treaty on Earth. If false, the uniform silence needs a better explanation.

## Documented realms

- [The Free Coasts](the-free-coasts.md) † — the godless-by-choice harbor league where east meets west

Hundreds more polities are unwritten — the region is deliberately the setting's
open frontier. Use the [location template](../../00-meta/templates/location.md).

## Contact with the three powers

Thin and mercantile, almost all of it through the Free Coasts.
[Syreth's](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/syreth.md) agents work the harbor
league openly — which, if the Far Accord exists, is either its permitted exception
or its slow violation.
