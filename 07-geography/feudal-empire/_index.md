# The Feudal Empire

**Core territories:** Europe, Mediterranean Lowlands, Anatolia, West Africa

## Government

A feudal hierarchy with the Emperor at its apex. [Great Houses](great-houses.md)
rule territorial holdings with significant autonomy. Nobility is hereditary. Social
mobility is limited but possible.

## Geography

- **European Heartland** — Atlantic coast to [the Wound's](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/the-wound.md) edge. **Thessalar** (pop. 6,000,000) is the capital — named for [Thessaly](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/thessaly.md), and sitting above the sealed god [Malevictus](../../04-divine-beings/sealed-gods/malevictus.md).
- **Mediterranean Lowlands** — partially drained inland sea. Exposed seabeds rich with minerals. **Lowgate** (pop. 600,000) is the resource-extraction center.
- **West African Holdings** — colonial territories. Extraction-focused economy, harsh "contracted labor" (see [Slavery & Labor](../../10-supplementary/slavery-and-labor.md)).

## Culture

Society built on the **"Chain of Service."** Heavy, layered fashion showing lineage.
Social taboo: **waste**.

[Vaelthrix](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/vaelthrix.md),
[Thessaly](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/thessaly.md),
[Mordrath](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/mordrath.md),
[Syreth](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/syreth.md); the minor god
[Seraphex](../../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/seraphex.md) rules an industrial
region. † Also honored: [Cindrath](../../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/cindrath.md)
in the eastern foundry country and [Brakka](../../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/brakka.md)
in the Mediterranean Lowlands.
