# The Great Houses

The Empire's territorial nobility. Each is one line of canon so far — every house is
an expansion target (promote to individual files as they grow).

## House Valdric
Northern. Military tradition. Patron: [Vaelthrix](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/vaelthrix.md).

## House Merentis
Southern coastal. Naval power. Patron: [Syreth](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/syreth.md).

## House Oranthos
Eastern mountains. Mining wealth. Close to [Mordrath](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/mordrath.md).

## House Thessian
Central. Political sophistication. Closest to [Thessaly](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/thessaly.md).

## House Kravenhold
Western frontier. Guards against [Wound](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/the-wound.md)
expansion. *(Canon calls Kravenhold the "western frontier" while the Wound lies on
the Empire's east — most plausibly it holds the Wound's western edge. Flagged in
[continuity notes](../../00-meta/continuity-notes.md).)*
