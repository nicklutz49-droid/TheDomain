# Geographic Overview

The world is divided among three major powers — the
[Feudal Empire](feudal-empire/_index.md), the [Federal Union](federal-union.md), and
the [Confederacy](confederacy.md) — with several independent or isolated regions:
the [Pale Sovereignty](pale-sovereignty.md), [the Silence](the-silence.md), and the
[neutral territories](neutral-territories.md).

The [Domain's stabilizing effect](../02-the-domain/the-domain.md) has transformed
global geography:

- Former deserts bloom — the Sahara is green; Arabia is fertile.
- The Mediterranean is **partially drained**, exposing mineral-rich seabeds.
- North America was reshaped with a massive inland sea.
- Climate zones have shifted toward hospitability wherever relays function properly — and toward hostility wherever they don't.

## The strategic picture

Cold war between the three powers (present era), fought through proxies under the
[Twelve's rules](../04-divine-beings/nature-of-divinity.md). Two wildcards sit
outside the balance: Palladia behind the Pale Maze, and the Silence, where no god
can reach at all.
