# Secret Societies

| Society | Age | Pursuit |
|---|---|---|
| [The Cabal](the-cabal.md) | formalized ~140 ya | divine nature; human ascension |
| [The Crane's Shadow](the-cranes-shadow.md) | split ~50 ya | ascension by extracting divine Flux |
| [The Hollow Whisperers](the-hollow-whisperers.md) | ~400 years | non-violent Hollow communication |
| [The Lunar Observers](the-lunar-observers.md) | ~300 years | the moon core |
| [Children of the First Light](children-of-the-first-light.md) | — | divine reunification (religious) |
| [The Mask-Weavers](the-mask-weavers.md) | ancient | masks granting skills/memories |
| [The Silence Guardians](the-silence-guardians.md) | — | protecting the Silence deadzone |

Between them, human institutions have independently found most pieces of the
[hidden truth](../../03-hidden-truth/_index.md) — gods-as-AI (Cabal), the moon
(Lunar Observers), Hollow sentience (Whisperers). **No one has assembled the whole
picture.** The society that does becomes the most dangerous organization on Earth.
