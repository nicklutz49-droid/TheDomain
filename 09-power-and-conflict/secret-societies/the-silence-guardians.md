# The Silence Guardians

Protecting [the Silence](../../07-geography/the-silence.md) deadzone. That is all
canon says.

## Open threads

- Protecting it from what — annexation, relay construction, or discovery of what makes it silent?
- If the deadzone was *deliberately created* (the region's standing suspicion), the Guardians may be its hereditary maintenance crew — the only humans continuously holding a pre-collapse secret.
- Guardians of a place where gods can't see are, by construction, invisible to divine surveillance. Perfect co-conspirators for anyone hiding from the Twelve.
