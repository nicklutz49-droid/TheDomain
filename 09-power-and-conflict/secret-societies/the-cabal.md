# The Cabal

The most significant secret society — dedicated to understanding divine nature and
achieving **human ascension**.

## What they know

- Gods are artificial.
- The [Domain](../../02-the-domain/the-domain.md) is technology.
- [God-script](../../02-the-domain/god-script.md) is programming (they've accumulated significant knowledge but lack the permissions to make it function fully).
- Humans [have ascended](../../04-divine-beings/ascended/_index.md).
- The [lunar complex](../../03-hidden-truth/lunar-complex.md) exists.

## What they don't know

- [The Entity](../../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md).
- [The alien threat](../../03-hidden-truth/the-shriven.md).
- How to achieve ascension reliably.

## Leadership

**Valdris Crane** — High Curator of the Meridian Archives. Secretly a
Shadow-affinity [Fluxborn](../../05-empowered-humans/fluxborn.md). His sister was
killed by Hollow **while a god watched** — the wound the whole organization grew
around.

## Structure

- **Outer Circle** — data gatherers.
- **Inner Circle** — operatives.
- **The Apex** — five individuals building **synthetic vessels for consciousness**.

## Current project: "The Hollow Key"

Attempting to tame a Class IV
[Hollow](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/hollow/_index.md). The timeline lists the
Cabal as "approaching critical experiments" — this is the present-day fuse.

## Who's watching (restricted)

[Thessaly](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/thessaly.md) knows the Cabal exists and
has watched Crane for decades **with amusement**.
