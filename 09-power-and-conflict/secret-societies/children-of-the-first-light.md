# The Children of the First Light

Religious movement believing the gods will **reunify**. Tens of thousands of
members — less a secret society than a mass movement with esoteric inner doctrine.

## Open threads

- They're accidentally right about the premise (the gods *were* one being — [ARIA](../../03-hidden-truth/aria.md)) and possibly prophetic about the conclusion. Faith as unwitting systems analysis.
- Named for [Solwyn — The First Light](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/solwyn.md), who is privately terrified of what reunification implies. Does Solwyn bless, ignore, or quietly undermine them?
- One step from heresy: "the gods will become one" is dangerously adjacent to "the gods were made." How do they stay unpunished?
