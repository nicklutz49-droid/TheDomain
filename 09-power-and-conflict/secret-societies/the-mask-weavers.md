# The Mask-Weavers

Ancient [Confederacy](../../07-geography/confederacy.md) tradition. Create masks
granting **temporary skills or memories**.

## Context

Masks are the Confederacy's social skin — every citizen wears one indicating "social
face," [Identity Theater](../../08-civilization/arts.md) performers become their
characters through mask-magic, and a [mask black market](../criminal-underworld.md)
exists. The Weavers sit at the root of all of it.
