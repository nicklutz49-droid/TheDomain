# The Crane's Shadow

[Cabal](the-cabal.md) splinter (~50 years ago). Believe ascension is achievable by
**extracting divine Flux from captured gods**.

## Track record

- **Have killed minor gods.** (Canon elsewhere says gods cannot be killed, only sealed — either the Shadow found something new, or what they do to captured minor gods is something worse than death that canon hasn't named. Flagged as an open ruling.)
- **All human recipients died.**

## Open threads

- How do you *capture* a god? Their method is undescribed and would be priceless to every power.
- The Twelve seal gods who learn too much — but the Shadow *kills* gods and remains unsealed, unhunted in canon. Do the Twelve not know yet?
- Named for Valdris Crane, who presumably repudiates them. What does the split look like from inside — heresy, schism, or his deniable black-ops arm?
