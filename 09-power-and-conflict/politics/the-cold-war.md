# The Cold War

Canon says the Modern Era is a cold war between the powers. These are its five live
flashpoints, ~2,096 AC.

## 1. The Lowgate Concessions

Union engineering guilds hold expiring leases on Imperial Mediterranean seabed
claims — the same dispute that lit the [Shatter War](../../01-foundation/events/the-shatter-war.md)
278 years ago, up for renewal again. The Empire wants the guilds out;
[Lowgate's](../../07-geography/feudal-empire/_index.md) extraction economy would
collapse without their expertise. [Syreth's](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/syreth.md)
brokers are somehow on retainer to both sides.

## 2. The Shatterbelt

The chain of micro-glitch-zones along the old war frontier is a de facto DMZ —
patrolled by both powers, mapped by neither honestly. Incidents rise annually:
salvage crews, Hollow surges, and lately *survey teams* found where survey teams
shouldn't be.

## 3. The Fluxborn pipeline

Fluxborn defect along the [Silk Passage](../../07-geography/neutral-territories.md)
in both directions, brokered by parties who advertise nowhere. Every defection
strains the [Accords](../../01-foundation/events/battle-of-red-sands.md); each
power's counterintelligence blames the others' recruiters, and all three are
correct.

## 4. The Pale Watch

The joint flotilla observing the [Pale Maze](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/the-pale-maze.md)
is the only place all three militaries anchor side by side — cooperation sustained
by shared fear and poisoned by the suspicion that someone will get *in* first.
[Ashka's](../../13-characters/ashka-of-the-pale.md) escape two years ago proved
the Maze can be threaded; every power now quietly funds maze-runners.

## 5. Shard certification

A tariff war over [Flux Shard](../../08-civilization/economics.md) certification:
whose assay-marks are honored where. Boring, bloodless, and the most likely of the
five to actually start something — trade wars starve borders that armies never
touch.

## The stabilizers

Working against all five: the [40% rule](../military.md) (mass war poisons the
ground it wins), the memory of the Grey Harvest, divine reluctance
([divine politics](divine-politics.md)), and the merchant lobby of three
currencies that all crash together.
