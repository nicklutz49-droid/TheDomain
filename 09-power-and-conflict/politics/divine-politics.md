# Divine Politics

The Twelve have factions the way weather has fronts — slow, vast, and dangerous at
the boundaries. Framework: the [Concordance rules](../../04-divine-beings/nature-of-divinity.md);
forum: the **Centenary Conclave**, held every hundred years since the rules were
sealed (seven years after the summit — Conclaves fall on years ending in 7 by the
[calendar](../../08-civilization/calendar.md)... in practice everyone says "the
century mark"). **The 22nd Conclave convenes in 2,100 AC — four years from now.**

## The standing docket

- **The Axiom question.** [Axiom](../../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/axiom.md) continues the investigation that got their namesake sealed. Preemptive sealing (Veranthos: "error-correction") vs. tolerance (Yrshen: "sealing curiosity is confessing"). Deadlocked for decades.
- **The watchlist.** [Seraphex](../../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/seraphex.md) (asking why power differs), [Velthari](../../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/velthari.md) (uncatchable, knows most), and — newly, quietly — [Vesperine](../../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/vesperine.md), whose record-audit is closing on the [Verantum edits](../../01-foundation/events/the-verantum-ascension.md).
- **The Pale.** Does the Concordance bind [Palladia](../../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/palladia.md)? She never signed; she now rivals the signatories. If the Maze opens, the rules offer no answer — which is itself the answer nobody says aloud.
- **The escape audit.** Someone has been helping near-sealing minor gods vanish for centuries. Everyone knows it's [Yrshen](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/yrshen.md). Proving it means asking what "no direct combat" permits as enforcement — a door no one wants opened.
- **The Watcher's reports.** [Ithrazel](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/ithrazel.md) files accounts of *communicating* Hollow. The Twelve thank the Watcher and table the reports. The reports are getting shorter.

## The undercurrents

- [Naveris](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/naveris.md) is repositioning — worship infrastructure shifting seaward, favors called in coastal cities. Her math says the balance fails within a millennium; her movements say she's not waiting.
- [Syreth](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/syreth.md) now holds influence in all three powers plus the [Free Coasts](../../07-geography/the-americas/the-free-coasts.md). At what point does hedging become a position?
- [Kythera's](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/kythera.md) memory gaps are noticed. Her refusal of the [Emperor's healing](imperial-succession.md) rattled allies who can't decide if it was principle or symptom.
- **The Americas abstention.** The western god-kings never joined and are never discussed — a silence so uniform it reads as agreement. Rumor names it the **Far Accord**: mutual ignorance, sworn early. ([Tezcamotl's](../../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/tezcamotl.md) new fixation on the moon may test it.)

## The Conclave in four years

Expected flashpoints: the Axiom vote, the Pale contingency, Naveris's movements —
and the item no agenda lists: at each of the last three Conclaves,
[Whisper](../../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/whisper.md) has appeared, uninvited,
exactly once, said nothing, and left. The Twelve pretend this doesn't happen.
