# The Union Assembly

The **Concord Assembly** — forty national delegations, one chamber in Verantum —
governs the [Federal Union](../../07-geography/federal-union.md) by weighted
consensus. Its chair, the **First Arbiter**, is elected from among sitting
delegates for a seven-year term, twice renewable.
[Ilhan Reza](../../13-characters/ilhan-reza.md) is in the final year of his second.

## The blocs

- **The Meridian bloc** — the core cities. Merit orthodoxy: the Exam is sacred, the system works, don't touch it. Strongest in the bureaucracy and the [Architect Corps](../military.md).
- **The Green Heart bloc** — the agrarian nations. Wants Exam reform (rural candidates test poorly and feed the Union anyway), invokes [Anverra](../../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/anverra.md) in every session, and has the votes to matter for the first time in a generation.
- **The Frontier bloc** — glitch-border states. Security first: more Glitch Specialists, more funding for the [Scar of Voices](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/the-scar-of-voices.md) containment, less Verantum oversight.

## The Transparency Crisis

A leaked audit of the [Dream Archive](../../10-supplementary/prophecies-and-sacred-texts.md)
showed that a portion of verified-predictive dreams were **classified upward** —
the public record of prophecy is *curated*. In a society whose taboo is secrecy,
this is a constitutional wound. The **Freedom Network** amplifies every leak; the
Meridian bloc calls it foreign-funded; the Frontier bloc quietly agrees with the
leakers; and nobody has yet asked the worst question — *curated by whom, and are
the withheld dreams about anything in particular?*

## The succession

Reza cannot run again. Leading candidates: **Examiner-General Nadia Osei**
(Meridian, incorruptible, currently sitting on an exam-fraud investigation that
reaches into her own bloc) and **Delegate Yusuf Kalla** of the Green Heart
(reformer, Anverra-blessed at his confirmation, which the Meridian bloc considers
a stunt).
