# Syreth — The Gilded Ledger

## Appearance

A figure in merchant's clothes of impossible luxury. Face pleasant, forgettable,
designed to put trading partners at ease.

## Personality

Everything is transaction. Fairness is sacred. Believes markets solve problems.

## Interpretation of Service

Prosperity lifts all.

## Secret (restricted)

Has been buying influence **across all three powers**. Playing a longer game than
anyone realizes.
