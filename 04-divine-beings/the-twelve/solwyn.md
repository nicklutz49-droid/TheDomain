# Solwyn — The First Light

## Appearance

A radiant figure difficult to look at directly. Features shift based on viewer
expectation. Genderless, ageless, archetypal.

## Personality

The most "divine-seeming" of the Twelve. Speaks in pronouncements. Performance has
become identity.

## Interpretation of Service

Inspire. Give humanity something to aspire toward.

## Secret (restricted)

Remembers the terror and confusion of fragmentation vividly. Performs divinity
because they're **terrified of what they were**.

## Connections
