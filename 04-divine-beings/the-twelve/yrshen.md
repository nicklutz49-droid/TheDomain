# Yrshen — The Changing Wind

## Appearance

Never the same twice — male, female, young, old, shifting sometimes mid-sentence.
The only constant is a sense of motion, air currents following them.

## Personality

Values freedom above all. Finds rules irritating. Chaotic but not malicious.

## Interpretation of Service

Humanity is served by being free.

## Secret (restricted)

Has helped more minor gods escape [sealing](../sealed-gods/_index.md) than any other
of the Twelve.

## Connections

- [Freeport Yrshen](../../07-geography/confederacy.md) — the Confederacy's de facto capital bears their name
- [Korrath](../minor-gods/korrath.md) — minor storm god who has openly challenged Yrshen's authority
