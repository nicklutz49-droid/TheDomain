# Veranthos — The Measured

## Appearance

A tall, androgynous figure in layered robes that shift between gray and pale blue.
Face is calm to the point of unsettling — emotions register seconds after they
should. Eyes are pure white with no visible pupil.

## Personality

Deliberate, patient, speaks in carefully constructed sentences. Genuinely believes
in collective governance and slow progress.

## Interpretation of Service

Humanity thrives through stable institutions. Rushing creates errors.

## Domain Affinity

God-script manipulation and Domain perception. Has mapped more
[god-script](../../02-the-domain/god-script.md) syntax than any other of the Twelve.

## Connections

- [Federal Union](../../07-geography/federal-union.md) — aligned power; Veranthos's temperament pervades Union institutions
- [Calister](calister.md) — fellow Union god whose recklessness with god-script Veranthos would presumably not approve of
