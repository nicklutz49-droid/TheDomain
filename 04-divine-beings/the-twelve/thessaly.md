# Thessaly — The Veiled Throne

## Appearance

A regal woman in Imperial court dress shifting between eras. Face always partially
obscured. What's visible is strikingly beautiful.

## Personality

Sophisticated, manipulative, views politics as the highest art. Genuinely enjoys the
game of power.

## Interpretation of Service

Humanity needs structure and hierarchy.

## Secret (restricted)

Knows the [Cabal](../../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-cabal.md) exists.
Has watched Valdris Crane for decades **with amusement**.

## Connections

- [House Thessian](../../07-geography/feudal-empire/great-houses.md) — the Empire's central political house, closest to her
- [Thessalar](../../07-geography/feudal-empire/_index.md) — the Imperial capital bears her name
- [Order of the Veiled Throne](../../09-power-and-conflict/military.md) — intelligence/assassination knightly order
