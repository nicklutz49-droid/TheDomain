# Naveris — The Deep Current

## Appearance

A figure made of water given human shape — transparent in certain lights, edges
rippling. Voice sounds like it's coming from underwater.

## Personality

Calm, adaptive, goes around obstacles. Patient but not passive.

## Interpretation of Service

Flow where needed. Adapt.

## Secret (restricted)

Has calculated that the current balance **cannot hold another thousand years**.
Positioning to survive.
