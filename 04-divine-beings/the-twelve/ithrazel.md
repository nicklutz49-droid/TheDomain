# Ithrazel — The Hollow Watcher

## Appearance

A gaunt figure in tattered robes. Face is a smooth mask with two dark pits for eyes.
Surrounded by perpetual unease.

## Personality

Withdrawn, speaks rarely, fascinated by
[Hollow](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/hollow/_index.md) in disturbing ways.

## Interpretation of Service

Watch the boundaries. Guard against what breaks through.

## Secret (restricted)

Has communicated with [persistent Hollow](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/hollow/persistent/_index.md)
entities. Suspects they're fragments of something trying to exist.

## Connections

- [The Hollow King](../minor-gods/the-hollow-king.md) — a minor god becoming Hollow; squarely Ithrazel's problem
- [Chorrus](../sealed-gods/chorrus.md) — sealed for the fascination Ithrazel still indulges
- [The Hollow Whisperers](../../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-hollow-whisperers.md) — human society attempting what Ithrazel has already done
