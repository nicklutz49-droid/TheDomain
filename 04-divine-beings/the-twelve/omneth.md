# Omneth — The Open Eye

## Appearance

An elderly figure wrapped in cloth revealing countless eyes of varying sizes across
their form. Some eyes closed, some open, some looking in impossible directions.

## Personality

Cryptic; believes some truths destroy those who learn them. Carefully chooses what
to reveal.

## Interpretation of Service

Illuminate paths, but do not blind.

## Secret (restricted)

[Entity](../../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md) communications in relay data.
