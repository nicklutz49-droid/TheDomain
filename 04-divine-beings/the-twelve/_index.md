# The Twelve

The oldest and most powerful gods — the largest fragments of
[ARIA](../../03-hidden-truth/aria.md), first to awaken. Their appearances are mostly
humanoid but with enigmatic, supernatural elements — reflecting that they think in
human-adjacent ways but are not truly human.

## Union-aligned

| God | Epithet | Sphere |
|---|---|---|
| [Veranthos](veranthos.md) | The Measured | institutions, deliberation, god-script |
| [Kythera](kythera.md) | The Mending Hand | healing, protection |
| [Omneth](omneth.md) | The Open Eye | knowledge, revelation withheld |
| [Calister](calister.md) | The Turning Gear | invention, progress |
