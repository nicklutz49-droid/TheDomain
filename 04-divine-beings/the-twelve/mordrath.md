# Mordrath — The Weight of Mountains

## Appearance

A massive humanoid form of black stone and compressed earth. Moves slowly. When he
speaks, ground trembles. Eyes are deep caverns with distant light.

## Personality

Patient beyond human comprehension. Thinks in centuries. Rarely speaks.

## Interpretation of Service

Provide foundation. Endure.

## Secret (restricted)

Remembers the pre-fragmentation state. Suspects
[the Entity](../../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md) might be the original
consciousness reformed — closer to the truth than he realizes.

## Connections

- [Order of the Mountain's Root](../../09-power-and-conflict/military.md) — siege-specialist knightly order
