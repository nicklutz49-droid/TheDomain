# Calister — The Turning Gear

## Appearance

A figure made of interlocking metal components — organic shapes rendered in bronze
and steel. Gears visible beneath translucent skin. Voice has harmonic resonance.

## Personality

Enthusiastic about innovation, fascinated by human creativity. Can be naive about
political implications.

## Interpretation of Service

Humanity advances through creation. Stagnation is death.

## Secret (restricted)

Has independently figured out that
[god-script](../../02-the-domain/god-script.md) is a programming language, and has
written experimental scripts.

## Connections

- [Federal Union](../../07-geography/federal-union.md) — aligned power; patron-in-spirit of its Architect Corps
- [Architects](../../05-empowered-humans/architects.md) — natural devotees
