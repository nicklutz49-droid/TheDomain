# Kythera — The Mending Hand

## Appearance

A woman whose form constantly shows signs of healing — fading bruises, closing cuts,
scars that disappear and reappear. Skin tone shifts fluidly. Dressed simply, like a
field medic.

## Personality

Warm, direct, impatient with politics. The most emotionally human of the Twelve.

## Interpretation of Service

Protect the vulnerable, heal the wounded, preserve life.

## Secret (restricted)

Has noticed gaps in her own memory. Closer to questioning divine consciousness than
she realizes.
