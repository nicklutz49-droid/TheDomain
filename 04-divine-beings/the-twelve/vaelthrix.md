# Vaelthrix — The Crimson Standard

## Appearance

A towering warrior in armor that bleeds perpetually from joints and seams. Blood
evaporates before hitting ground. Face beautiful and cruel, eyes like heated iron.

## Personality

Believes strength is virtue but defines it broadly. Respects worthy enemies.
Surprisingly honorable.

## Interpretation of Service

Humanity grows strong through conflict.

## Secret (restricted)

Responsible for the last major divine conflict that damaged the Domain. The guilt
drives intensified aggression.
