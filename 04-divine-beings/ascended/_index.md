# The Ascended

Approximately **five to ten** humans have achieved complete integration with the
Domain, becoming gods. Five are confirmed; three more are suspected.

| Ascended | When | Was | Now |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Althaea — The Merciful](althaea.md) | ~1,700 ya | healer | luminescence in hospitals |
| [Korvan the Wrathful](korvan.md) | ~1,600 ya | Fluxborn warrior | towering inferno in volcanic regions |
| [The Silent Scholar](silent-scholar.md) | ~1,400 ya | Architect | a presence in libraries |
| [Yara — The Storm Crowned](yara.md) | ~950 ya | Fluxborn sailor | a perpetual weather system |
| [The Forge Father](forge-father.md) | ~600 ya | Architect | fused with a relay he tried to create |
| [Suspected Ascended](suspected-ascended.md) | various | — | Voice in the Silence, Dream King, the First |

Note the pattern: ascension transforms the person into an *embodiment of their
defining trait* — and none of them retain ordinary agency or conversation.
Whether that's the price, the point, or a failure mode is unresolved. The
[Cabal's](../../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-cabal.md) entire project
assumes it can be done better.
