# Yara — The Storm Crowned

A [Fluxborn](../../05-empowered-humans/fluxborn.md) sailor who merged with a
legendary storm, ~950 years ago. Now travels the oceans as a perpetual weather
system.

## Open threads

- Sailors must track her like weather — is meeting Yara a blessing, a hazard, or both? (Compare [Domain storms](../../10-supplementary/weather.md).)
- Shares the epithet "Storm Crown(ed)" with the minor god [Korrath](../minor-gods/korrath.md) — coincidence, rivalry, or naming tradition?
- Overlaps [Naveris's](../the-twelve/naveris.md) domain; the Deep Current tolerates a human-born storm in her waters.
