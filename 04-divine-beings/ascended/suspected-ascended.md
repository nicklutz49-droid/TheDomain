# Suspected / Unconfirmed Ascended

Three cases with credible claims and no confirmation:

## The Voice in the Silence

Some in [the Silence](../../07-geography/the-silence.md) deadzone report hearing a
voice offering guidance. Problem: the Domain's field doesn't reach the Silence, and
Ascended *are* Domain — so either the reports are false, the deadzone isn't total,
or the Voice is something else entirely.

## The Dream King

A figure appearing in dreams across all powers, offering prophecy. Overlaps
suspiciously with [the Entity's](../../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md) dream
manipulation — an Ascended, an Entity mask (compare
[Whisper](../minor-gods/whisper.md)), or a genuine third party?

## The First

Legend says someone ascended **within years of the collapse**, before the gods fully
awakened. If true, ascension predates every institution that now claims to
understand it — and possibly predates the sealing of
[the Unnamed](../sealed-gods/the-unnamed.md).
