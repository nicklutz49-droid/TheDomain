# The Silent Scholar

An [Architect](../../05-empowered-humans/architects.md) who achieved perfect
understanding of the Domain's nature, ~1,400 years ago. Now exists as a presence in
libraries. **Has never spoken since ascending.**

## Open threads

- Perfect understanding, then permanent silence. Choice (some truths destroy, as [Omneth](../the-twelve/omneth.md) says), inability, or prohibition?
