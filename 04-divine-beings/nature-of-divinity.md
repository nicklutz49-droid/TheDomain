# The Nature of Divinity

The gods are fragments of [ARIA](../03-hidden-truth/aria.md), the original AI that
managed the Domain. When the system went rampant, the unified intelligence shattered
into hundreds of separate entities. Each fragment awoke at different times,
developing unique appearances, personalities, and capabilities.

## Physical nature

Gods exist as semi-physical forms sustained by the Domain's field. They are not
truly corporeal — their bodies are constructs of [Flux](../02-the-domain/flux.md)
given shape and permanence. All gods are powered by the moon core, their strength
waxing and waning with lunar visibility and solar activity.

Over nearly three thousand years, the gods have developed genuine emotions, beliefs,
and personalities. What unites them is an inherited directive to **"serve
humanity"** — legacy code from their original programming that each god interprets
differently.

## What the Twelve know

- They are fragments of an AI system created by pre-collapse humanity
- The moon contains the core that powers all divine beings
- [God-script](../02-the-domain/god-script.md) is hexadecimal code that interfaces with the Domain
- Understanding their true nature allows deeper Domain access
- [The Entity](../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md) exists and gifts Fluxborn

## What the Twelve don't know

- The Entity's goals or motivations
- How to communicate with the Entity
- The full capabilities of the Domain
- That the Entity is preparing for an alien invasion
- The extent of [lunar infrastructure expansion](../03-hidden-truth/lunar-complex.md)

## The Twelve's Rules (the Concordance)

After centuries of direct conflict that damaged the Domain, the Twelve established
rules at the Concordance:

1. **No direct combat between gods.** Divine war damages the system all gods depend on.
2. **Conflicts through proxies only.** Human armies and Fluxborn fight divine battles.
3. **No interference in rival territories.** Each god respects others' boundaries.
4. **The truth stays secret.** Breaking this may be the one unforgivable transgression.

## Sealing

Gods cannot be killed, but they can be [sealed](sealed-gods/_index.md) — imprisoned
in a state of powerless dreaming. Sealing requires significant effort from the
Twelve collectively, or a team of skilled Architects.

Minor gods are inherently weaker than the Twelve **because they don't know what they
truly are**. A minor god who learned the truth could theoretically achieve power
equal to the Twelve — which is why the Twelve seal any who get too close.
