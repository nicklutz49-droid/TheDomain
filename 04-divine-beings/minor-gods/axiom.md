# Axiom — The Question

A figure made of geometric shapes constantly reconfiguring. Has been systematically
investigating divine nature **for centuries**.

## Danger

The Twelve have debated sealing them preemptively. The current Axiom took the name
as tribute to [Truth-Seeker Axiom-1](../sealed-gods/axiom-1.md), sealed ~275 years
ago for discovering too much — so this is the *second* god on this exact path.
