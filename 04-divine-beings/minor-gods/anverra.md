# Anverra — The Gathered Sheaf

Goddess of harvest and provision in the Union's **Green Heart**. Appears as a
broad-shouldered woman of indeterminate age with chaff in her hair and ledger-ink
on her fingers — a farmer who audits.

## Personality

Practical, generous, allergic to ceremony. Answers prayers about drainage more
readily than prayers about destiny. Distrusts cities on principle but keeps
Verantum fed anyway.

## Interpretation of Service

Nobody serves an empty stomach. Feed them first; argue theology after.

## Worship and territory

Granary-shrines across the Green Heart; her priests double as the Union's crop
auditors and famine-insurance clerks. Her festival is the first cut of the harvest,
when the year's yield predictions are read aloud.

## Secret (restricted)

Her yield predictions are *too* accurate — decades of perfect calls. Anverra
believes it is craft. In truth she is unconsciously reading relay telemetry through
the Domain, parsing data she has no idea she can access. A sufficiently curious
[Union statistician](../../07-geography/federal-union.md) comparing her predictions
to relay maintenance schedules would find a correlation no theology explains.
