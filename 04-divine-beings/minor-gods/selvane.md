# Selvane — The Turning Season

Goddess of the monsoon and the agricultural year in the Confederacy's Ganges
territories. Appears as a tall woman whose clothing is always the *coming* season —
rain-heavy silks in the dry months, sun-bleached cotton before the rains.

## Personality

Serene, cyclical, comfortable with endings. Preaches that nothing is lost, only
returned — the Confederacy's debt logic applied to weather.

## Interpretation of Service

Time is a wheel and humanity rides it. Keep the wheel turning smoothly.

## Worship and territory

Her calendar-priests fix the planting dates for the Ganges breadbasket; the
[Confederacy's seasonal month-names](../../08-civilization/calendar.md) descend
from her liturgy. The festival of **Ledger's Turning** — debts recited and
selectively forgiven at year's end — is co-celebrated in her name and
[Syreth's](../the-twelve/syreth.md), to both gods' irritation.

## Secret (restricted)

Her monsoon has been arriving **one day earlier each decade** for the last two
centuries. Twenty days of drift, metronome-regular, and she cannot account for it —
weather in her territory obeys her, except for this. She has told no one.
*(GM-only: the [Entity](../../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md) is incrementally
re-tuning global weather patterns as part of pre-arrival agricultural optimization.
Selvane's monsoon is the visible edge of a planetary adjustment.)*
