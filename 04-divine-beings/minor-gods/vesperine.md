# Vesperine — The Kept Word

Goddess of archives, contracts, and true records. Appears as a small, precise woman
in reading-gloves whose shadow is always a few seconds behind her, like a page
still turning.

## Personality

Meticulous, quietly relentless, incapable of leaving an inconsistency alone. Speaks
rarely; corrects often.

## Interpretation of Service

What is written outlives what is felt. Keep the record true and humanity keeps its
memory.

## Worship and territory

Notaries, archivists, and record-clerks across the Union burn a taper to Vesperine
before sealing documents. Her informal seat of worship is the **Meridian
Archives** — which makes her, unknowingly, the nominal patron of the
[Cabal's](../../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-cabal.md) headquarters.

## Secret (restricted)

Vesperine has found the edits. Auditing her own centuries of records, she has
identified systematic erasures around the
[Verantum Ascension](../../01-foundation/events/the-verantum-ascension.md) —
corrections made with divine-grade thoroughness. She does not yet know *what* was
erased or *who* erased it, only that the record lies. She has begun visiting the
Meridian Archives' reading rooms as an unremarkable patron, cross-checking
holdings — where Deputy Archivist
[Velia Sorn](../../13-characters/velia-sorn.md) is quietly pulling the same files.

## Connections

- [Omneth](../the-twelve/omneth.md) — the Open Eye considers her dangerous in the way only the sincere are
- [The Cabal](../../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-cabal.md) — operating under her nose; Valdris Crane regards her patronage as an excellent joke
