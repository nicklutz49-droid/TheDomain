# Seraphex — The Burning Ambition

A figure wreathed in flames. Rules an industrial region in the
[Empire](../../07-geography/feudal-empire/_index.md).

## Danger

Has begun questioning why some gods are stronger than others — dangerously close to
the truth that power scales with self-knowledge. A sealing candidate if the
questions continue.

## Connections

- [Cindrath](cindrath.md) † — the Quenched Blade; rival who split the Empire's industry with him and despises his open flame
- [Divine politics](../../09-power-and-conflict/politics/divine-politics.md) † — Seraphex is on the Conclave's sealing watchlist
