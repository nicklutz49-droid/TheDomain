# Minor Gods

Over **three hundred** minor gods exist. They are inherently less powerful than the
Twelve because they don't know what they truly are. If a minor god learned the
truth, they could theoretically achieve power equal to the Twelve — which is why the
Twelve [seal](../sealed-gods/_index.md) any who get too close.

Minor gods appear more human-like than the Twelve because they fully believe
themselves to be elevated beings, presenting as beautiful or impressive humans with
minimal supernatural markers.
