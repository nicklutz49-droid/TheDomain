# Cindrath — The Quenched Blade

God of forges, tempering, and finished work in the Empire's eastern industrial
belt. Appears as a soot-dark smith with cooling-cracks of dim orange across his
skin — heat *contained*, in deliberate contrast to
[Seraphex's](seraphex.md) open flame.

## Personality

Taciturn, exacting, contemptuous of ornament. Believes a thing is finished when
nothing more can be *removed*. Keeps his temper the way he keeps his forge: banked,
never out.

## Interpretation of Service

Strength is not the fire. Strength is what survives the quench.

## Worship and territory

Foundry towns in the [Oranthos](../../07-geography/feudal-empire/great-houses.md)
mountains; smiths' guilds hang his mark over the quench-trough. His priests certify
weapons and tools — a Cindrath-stamped blade commands double price.

## The Rivalry

Cindrath and [Seraphex](seraphex.md) split the Empire's industry between them —
Seraphex the furnaces and ambition, Cindrath the finishing and restraint — and
despise each other with the intimacy of adjoining parishes. House Oranthos plays
them against each other for concessions.

## Secret (restricted)

Since the massacre at Corvenna Bridge (~30 ya, an
[Order of the Crimson Dawn](../../09-power-and-conflict/military.md) action against
striking foundry workers), Cindrath has quietly refused to bless weapons destined
for the Crimson Dawn — flaws emerge in the steel, always deniable. He is defying
[Vaelthrix](../the-twelve/vaelthrix.md) in increments and knows exactly how
dangerous that is.
