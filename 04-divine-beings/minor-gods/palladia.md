# Palladia the Eternal — God-Queen of the Pale

A minor god trapped inside the [Pale Maze](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/the-pale-maze.md)
when it formed ~200 years ago. Over two centuries of isolation she has transformed
into something no longer truly humanoid — a towering figure of white light and
crystalline structure.

## Why she matters

- Has achieved **power rivaling the Twelve** — which, per the rules of divinity, strongly implies she has learned something about her true nature. **She may know the truth.**
- Rules the [Pale Sovereignty](../../07-geography/pale-sovereignty.md) (5–8 million people) as an absolute theocracy that teaches the outside world as myth.
- If the barriers fell, conflict between Palladia and the Twelve could devastate civilizations.
