# Whisper — The Unnamed

Nobody agrees on what Whisper looks like. Appears to those in desperate need.

## The question

**May be connected to [the Entity](../../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md) somehow.**
The Entity supposedly communicates with no one — if Whisper is its voice, mask, or
unwitting instrument, that changes everything. The source deliberately leaves this
unresolved; see [continuity notes](../../00-meta/continuity-notes.md).
