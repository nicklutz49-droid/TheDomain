# Tezcamotl — The Obsidian Mirror

God-king of the [Mirror Throne](../../07-geography/the-americas/mirror-throne.md),
a mountain realm on the western shore of the Inland Sea. One of the divine rulers
of the Americas who never joined the Concordance and whom the Twelve have never
confronted. Appears as a tall figure whose face is a sheet of polished obsidian
reflecting the viewer — worshippers say Tezcamotl has every face and none.

## Personality

Watchful, ceremonious, coldly curious. Rules by *seeing*: nothing in his realm is
private, and his subjects are taught this is love.

## Interpretation of Service

To serve humanity is to know it completely. What is seen can be protected. What
hides, suffers alone.

## Rule

Every subject of the Mirror Throne carries a shard of obsidian mirror; through them
Tezcamotl scries his realm. Tribute is paid in **mirror-tithe** — hours spent
seated before the great Mirror, being *thoroughly seen*. Crime is rare, privacy
nonexistent, and the border is one-way for most.

## Secret (restricted)

For roughly a decade, Tezcamotl has turned his great Mirror on **the moon** — night
after night, obsessively. He has seen something in its reflection that he refuses to
describe: a flicker of *industry* where there should be stillness. He is closer to
observing the [lunar expansion](../../03-hidden-truth/lunar-complex.md) than any
being on Earth, and he wasn't even looking for it.

## Connections

- [The Americas](../../07-geography/the-americas/_index.md) — the Far Accord's most powerful signatory, if the Far Accord exists
- [Grandmother Cedar](grandmother-cedar.md) — his northern neighbor and philosophical opposite; their border is the quietest armed frontier on Earth
- [The Free Coasts](../../07-geography/the-americas/the-free-coasts.md) — his window for eastern trade; his mirror-shards are smuggled out as luxury spy-glass
