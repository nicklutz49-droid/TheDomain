# The Hollow King

A figure made of contained void. Lives at the edge of a
[glitch zone](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/_index.md) and has begun
to change.

## Danger

Might be the only god who truly understands
[Hollow](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/hollow/_index.md) — **because they're
becoming one.**

## Connections

- [Ithrazel](../the-twelve/ithrazel.md) — the Twelve's Hollow-watcher; the King is his logical case study or his failure
- [Chorrus](../sealed-gods/chorrus.md) — precedent: a god whose Hollow fascination ended in fusion and sealing
