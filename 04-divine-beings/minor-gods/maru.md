# Maru — The Thousand Wakes

God of harbors, departures, and the luck of the outbound voyage. Worshipped in
every [Confederacy](../../07-geography/confederacy.md) port from Freeport Yrshen to
the Thousand Islands. Appears as a barefoot, laughing sailor of shifting ancestry
whose wet footprints never dry.

## Personality

Charming, transactional, sentimental about ships and ruthless about cargo.
Remembers every vessel ever named in his honor and holds a grudge on behalf of
each one wrecked.

## Interpretation of Service

Every departure is a promise to return. Hold humanity to its promises.

## Worship and territory

Departure offerings — a coin, a lock of hair, a debt-token — thrown from the stern.
Harbormasters keep his ledger-shrines. He is junior to
[Naveris](../the-twelve/naveris.md) in every formal sense and cheerfully ignores
this.

## Secret (restricted)

Maru takes offerings from the **Gray Fleet**. Pirate crews tithe him a share of
every prize, and he accepts — in his accounting, piracy that spares the ship is
just aggressive salvage. Naveris knows and has said nothing yet; Admiral
[Kesari Ondo's](../../13-characters/kesari-ondo.md) quiet détente with the Gray
Fleet was brokered in one of his shrine-houses.

## Connections

- [Naveris](../the-twelve/naveris.md) — his superior, increasingly preoccupied with her own survival math
- [The Gray Fleet](../../09-power-and-conflict/criminal-underworld.md) — devoted congregants, to everyone's embarrassment
- [Yara the Storm Crowned](../ascended/yara.md) — sailors pray to Maru *about* Yara; he finds this professionally awkward
