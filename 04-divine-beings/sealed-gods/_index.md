# Sealed Gods

Gods cannot be killed, but they can be **sealed** — imprisoned in a state of
powerless dreaming. Sealing requires significant effort from the Twelve
collectively, or a team of skilled [Architects](../../05-empowered-humans/architects.md)
(first achieved by [Dren Ashford](../../05-empowered-humans/legendary-figures/dren-ashford.md),
~800 years ago).

| God | Sealed | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| [The Unnamed](the-unnamed.md) | unknown (oldest records) | erased from all records |
| [Vexis and Malum](vexis-and-malum.md), the Twin Terrors | ~1,200 ya | interpreted "serve humanity" as "rule absolutely" |
| [Malevictus](malevictus.md), the Crimson Heretic | ~800 ya | discovered partial truth; tried to spread it |
| [Chorrus](chorrus.md), the Hollow Consort | ~550 ya | Hollow worship, then fusion |
| [Truth-Seeker Axiom-1](axiom-1.md) | ~275 ya | discovered too much; sealed preemptively |

Note the pattern: half the sealings are truth-suppression, not justice. See
[The Nature of Divinity](../nature-of-divinity.md).
