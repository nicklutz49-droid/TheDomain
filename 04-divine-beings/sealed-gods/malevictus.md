# Malevictus — The Crimson Heretic

**Sealed ~800 years ago**, in the event remembered as the Crimson Apostasy.

Discovered a partial truth about divine nature and went mad trying to spread it.
Imprisoned beneath [Thessalar](../../07-geography/feudal-empire/_index.md) — the
Imperial capital sits on top of a sealed god.

## Legacy

- The sealing was led by [Dren Ashford](../../05-empowered-humans/legendary-figures/dren-ashford.md) — the first confirmed sealing by Architects without divine assistance.
- [The Malevictus Fragments](../../10-supplementary/prophecies-and-sacred-texts.md) — surviving writings; reading them without preparation causes psychological damage.
