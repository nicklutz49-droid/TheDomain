# Truth-Seeker Axiom-1

**Sealed ~275 years ago**, during the era of the Shatter War.

Independently discovered too much about divine nature. Sealed **preemptively** — not
for anything done, but for what was known. The current
[Axiom — The Question](../minor-gods/axiom.md) took the name as tribute.

## Final statement

Preserved among [forbidden texts](../../10-supplementary/prophecies-and-sacred-texts.md):

> "Look at the moon. Look at the moon. Look at the moon."
