# The Shatter War

The last great war between the powers — the reason the present is a cold war.

## Canon basis

The source timeline places the Shatter War ~275 ya, alongside the
[Dreamweaver's](../../05-empowered-humans/legendary-figures/the-dreamweaver.md)
sacrifice and [Axiom-1's](../../04-divine-beings/sealed-gods/axiom-1.md) sealing.
No combatants or course are given.

## Expanded detail (draft)

- **The trigger:** the Lowgate mineral concessions — Union engineering guilds held expiring leases on Imperial seabed claims — compounded by a cascade of Fluxborn defections through the Silk Passage that both sides called theft.
- **The fronts:** four years of grinding warfare along the Anatolian–Levantine border. Battlefield after battlefield crossed the 10,000-dead threshold, and the [40% rule](../../09-power-and-conflict/military.md) did its arithmetic: the border is now the **Shatterbelt**, a chain of micro-glitch-zones where the war's worst fields used to be. The war is named for what it left.
- **The nightmare plague:** in the war's third year, a plague of nightmare-born Hollow rose in [Kythera's Reach](../../07-geography/federal-union.md) — 1.2 million people dreaming war-grief in one city. The Dreamweaver ended it from inside the city's collective unconscious and did not come back.
- **The Grey Harvest:** the war effectively ended when a field of 14,000 fresh dead rose as a single [Procession](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/hollow/military-scale.md) and walked toward the nearest city, absorbing both armies' burial details on the way. The armistice was signed at Crossroads within the season.
- **The watchers:** divine proxies had escalated to the edge of the Concordance's limits — [Vaelthrix's](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/vaelthrix.md) orders and [Veranthos's](../../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/veranthos.md) Architect cohorts one incident from direct contact. Amid that unprecedented divine visibility, the minor god Axiom-1 gathered the data that got them sealed the following year.

## Consequences

The cold-war order; the Shatterbelt DMZ ([current flashpoint](../../09-power-and-conflict/politics/the-cold-war.md));
doctrine on all sides built to avoid mass pitched battle; the arms race migrating
into [Fluxborn recruitment](../../05-empowered-humans/fluxborn.md) instead of armies.
