# The Verantum Ascension

The most successful lie the Twelve ever told in public — and the birth certificate
of the Cabal.

## What the record says

In 1,946 AC, before two hundred thousand witnesses in Verantum's Audit Square, the
minor god Quorion the Honest Measure achieved voluntary unity with the divine and
ascended in robes of light. A holiday in three Union provinces still bears the
name.

## What happened (restricted)

[Quorion](../../04-divine-beings/sealed-gods/quorion.md) had publicly demonstrated
that his own miracles were reproducible mechanism — recreating a divine blessing
with inspectable Flux circuitry, as any god of standards would. The Twelve arrived
within the hour and sealed him where he stood, staging the sealing as an ascension.
The records were centrally corrected over the following decade.

## The leak

Three witness journals escaped correction — the **Threefold Testimony**: a
market-inspector's docket, a child's letter to her grandmother, and the private
shorthand of an off-duty Architect. The reading circles that formed around the
Testimony hardened, within a decade, into the formalized
[Cabal](../../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-cabal.md) (~140 ya —
matching the canon founding date).

## Present-day fallout

- [Vesperine](../../04-divine-beings/minor-gods/vesperine.md), goddess of true records, has found the edit-marks without knowing what they hide.
- Deputy Archivist [Velia Sorn](../../13-characters/velia-sorn.md) has begun pulling the same files at the Meridian Archives.
- One volume of the Threefold Testimony is missing from the Cabal's vaults — sold, decades ago, by a defector. Current holder unknown.

## Open threads
