# The Battle of Red Sands

Nine days in the Arabian margin that taught the powers what Fluxborn warfare
actually costs.

## Canon basis

Listed in the source timeline (~750 ya), otherwise undescribed. The
[Fluxborn Accords](../../09-power-and-conflict/military.md) — captured Fluxborn are
ransomed, not executed — exist in canon with no stated origin.

## Expanded detail (draft)

- **The sides:** the Empire's Third Expansion Legion against the Union's Second Mobilization, colliding at the southern margin of the [Burning Calm](../../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/the-burning-calm.md) during the Age of Expansion's last land grab.
- **The escalation:** on day four, each side executed captured Fluxborn rather than see them ransomed back into the fight. Eleven died on one side, nine on the other. Fluxborn on both sides stopped taking prisoners entirely.
- **The ground:** by day nine, enough blood and Flux had soaked the dunes that the ground began to glass over and *hum*. Both armies withdrew from what is now the **Glass Flats** — a minor glitch zone where a Static Swarm nests permanently, absent from the master zone table because no one contests it.
- **The Accords:** within a decade, marshal [Sahar of the Glass Banner](../../05-empowered-humans/legendary-figures/sahar-of-the-glass-banner.md) — who had refused the execution order — drove the Fluxborn Accords to signature at Haven's Rest.

## Consequences

The Accords ("honored in spirit, violated in practice" — canon); the Glass Flats;
Union precision doctrine (Sahar's legacy); a shared professional horror that held
for five centuries, until the [Shatter War](the-shatter-war.md).
