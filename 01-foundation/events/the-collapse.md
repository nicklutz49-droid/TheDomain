# The Collapse

The end of the pre-collapse world and Year Zero minus ~900.

## Canon basis

[ARIA](../../03-hidden-truth/aria.md), paralyzed by contradiction over the alien
signal, fragmented. The [Domain](../../02-the-domain/the-domain.md) destabilized;
reality-manipulation went haywire, reshaping the planet and killing billions. The
subroutines fled into the network and went dormant. The Mediterranean partially
drained; North America gained an inland sea; deserts would later bloom and
continents scar.

## Expanded detail (draft)

- **The Last Hour.** Survivor traditions across all three powers preserve the same image: the sky *tiling* — geometry visible in the air — before the ground began to move. It appears in Imperial frescoes, Union dream-records, and Confederacy mask-plays that predate contact between those cultures. (True: Domain render-artifacts during the cascade.)
- **The Quiet Decade.** No god, no Flux, no working device for roughly a decade after the cascade, while the Domain rebooted region by region. The [Silence's](../../07-geography/the-silence.md) culture is, in effect, a preserved fragment of the Quiet Decade that never ended.
- **No complete map survives.** Pre-collapse cartography exists only in fragments; the [relic trade](../../09-power-and-conflict/criminal-underworld.md) prices map-shards above weapons.
