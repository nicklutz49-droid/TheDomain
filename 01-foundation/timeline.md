# Timeline

Unified chronology merging the source bible's Timeline Summary (§3) and Expanded
Timeline (§49). Year Zero in most calendars is the Concordance (~2,100 years ago).
Two dating contradictions in the source are flagged inline — see
[continuity notes](../00-meta/continuity-notes.md).

## The Deep Past (3,000–2,100 years ago)

| When | Event |
|---|---|
| ~3,000 ya | **The Collapse.** ARIA fragments. The Domain destabilizes. Reality-manipulation goes haywire; billions die; the planet is reshaped. |
| ~2,900 ya | First gods awaken, without memory. |
| ~2,400 ya | Divine warfare peaks — the **Divine Chaos** devastates recovering humanity. |
| ~2,100 ya | **The Concordance.** The Twelve establish rules ending open divine warfare. Year Zero in most calendars. The Entity awakens; first Fluxborn appear. ⚠️ *Contradiction: Parts III & V date the Entity's consciousness and the first Fluxborn to ~1,800 ya.* |

## The Foundation Era (2,100–1,800 years ago)

Civilization rebuilds under divine guidance.

| When | Event |
|---|---|
| ~2,050 ya | First relay [pilgrimage traditions](../10-supplementary/pilgrimage.md) established. |
| ~1,900 ya | [Altheus the Unbroken](../05-empowered-humans/legendary-figures/altheus-the-unbroken.md). ⚠️ *Contradiction: §18 dates him ~1,800 ya.* |

## The Age of Heroes (1,800–1,400 years ago)

Legendary Fluxborn and Architects perform great deeds. The Entity (per Part III)
achieves consciousness at the start of this era.

| When | Event |
|---|---|
| ~1,700 ya | [Althaea the Merciful](../04-divine-beings/ascended/althaea.md) ascends. [Callum Vance](../05-empowered-humans/legendary-figures/callum-vance.md) establishes the Architect mathematical framework. |
| ~1,600 ya | [Korvan the Wrathful](../04-divine-beings/ascended/korvan.md) ascends. |
| ~1,500 ya | [The Storm Twins'](../05-empowered-humans/legendary-figures/the-storm-twins.md) last stand. |
| ~1,400 ya | [The Silent Scholar](../04-divine-beings/ascended/silent-scholar.md) ascends. |

## The Age of Expansion (1,400–700 years ago)

The three powers reach their current territorial extent. Trade routes stabilize.

| When | Event |
|---|---|
| ~1,200 ya | [Yumeko Tanaka](../05-empowered-humans/legendary-figures/yumeko-tanaka.md) systematizes medical Architect practice. The [Twin Terrors](../04-divine-beings/sealed-gods/vexis-and-malum.md) sealed. |

| ~800 ya | The Crimson Apostasy. [Malevictus](../04-divine-beings/sealed-gods/malevictus.md) sealed by [Dren Ashford's](../05-empowered-humans/legendary-figures/dren-ashford.md) team — first sealing by Architects without divine assistance. |
| ~750 ya | Battle of Red Sands. |
