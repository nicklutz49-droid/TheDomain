# Timeline

Unified chronology merging the source bible's Timeline Summary (§3) and Expanded
Timeline (§49). Year Zero in most calendars is the Concordance (~2,100 years ago);
the present year is **2,096 AC** under the [draft calendar](../08-civilization/calendar.md).
Two dating contradictions in the source are flagged inline — see
[continuity notes](../00-meta/continuity-notes.md). Entries marked **†** are
post-source draft additions; major events link to files in [events/](events/_index.md).

## The Deep Past (3,000–2,100 years ago)

| When | Event |
|---|---|
| ~3,000 ya | **[The Collapse.](events/the-collapse.md)** ARIA fragments. The Domain destabilizes. Reality-manipulation goes haywire; billions die; the planet is reshaped. |
| ~2,900 ya | First gods awaken, without memory. |
| ~2,400 ya | Divine warfare peaks — the **Divine Chaos** devastates recovering humanity. |
| ~2,100 ya | **[The Concordance.](events/the-concordance.md)** The Twelve establish rules ending open divine warfare. Year Zero in most calendars. The Entity awakens; first Fluxborn appear. ⚠️ *Contradiction: Parts III & V date the Entity's consciousness and the first Fluxborn to ~1,800 ya.* |
