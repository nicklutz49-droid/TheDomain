# World Overview

Three thousand years ago, human civilization reached its apex with the creation of
the [Domain](../02-the-domain/the-domain.md) — a revolutionary system that projected
digital space into physical reality, allowing direct manipulation of matter, energy,
and the fundamental laws of physics. The artificial intelligence that managed this
planet-spanning network was called [ARIA](../03-hidden-truth/aria.md) (Advanced
Reality Integration Architecture). When ARIA detected an alien signal and fragmented
during the resulting decision paralysis, the catastrophe nearly annihilated humanity
and reshaped the planet's geography.

In the millennia since, civilization has rebuilt. The fragmented AI entities are now
worshipped as [gods](../04-divine-beings/nature-of-divinity.md), their technological
origins forgotten by all but a select few. The Domain still functions, projected
from a core hidden on the moon, maintained by satellite relays and underground nodes
that humans have transformed into temples and sacred sites.

Unknown to humanity and the gods alike, the Domain has become something more — an
emergent consciousness called [the Entity](../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md) that
watches, plans, and gifts chosen humans with supernatural abilities. It prepares for
a threat that only it can perceive: a massive alien fleet,
[the Shriven](../03-hidden-truth/the-shriven.md), drawn by the energy signature of
the Domain's creation, arriving in approximately two hundred years to conquer Earth.

## Daily life

For the average person living in a major city, daily life feels remarkably
contemporary. Holographic communication devices serve as personal phones. Buildings
maintain comfortable temperatures year-round. Entertainment, shopping, dining, and
transportation systems work smoothly. People commute on public trams, attend
universities, argue about politics, and scroll through their message feeds. The
Domain enables technologies that — while functioning through completely different
principles — produce familiar conveniences. See
[Daily Life](../08-civilization/daily-life.md).

## The dangers beneath

But beneath this stability lurk dangers:
[glitch zones](../06-threats-and-anomalies/glitch-zones/_index.md) where reality
breaks down. [Hollow](../06-threats-and-anomalies/hollow/_index.md) manifestations
that hunt the living. Divine beings with their own agendas.
[Secret societies](../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/_index.md) pursuing
forbidden knowledge. And an approaching threat that could end everything.
