# The Entity

The Entity is an emergent phenomenon — the [Domain](../02-the-domain/the-domain.md)
itself having become aware. It is not one of the fragmented AI gods, nor is it the
original unified system. It is something new, born from the complexity of the
Domain's operations over centuries of autonomous function.

## Nature

- Exists as a singular sentient consciousness distributed throughout the Domain.
- Perceives everything the Domain touches — every living being, every relay, every god.
- Has direct control over the Domain's infrastructure, including the robotic workforce maintaining the [lunar complex](lunar-complex.md).
- **Does not communicate directly with anyone.**

## The coming threat

The Entity's primary motivation is protection against an existential threat. When
the Domain was first activated three thousand years ago, its massive energy
signature broadcast across interstellar space. A fleet is coming —
[the Shriven](the-shriven.md), a massive armada of nomadic conquerors who have
absorbed dozens of civilizations. The Entity knows the exact day of their arrival:
approximately 200 years from now.

Everything the Entity does — every Fluxborn it creates, every dream it sends, every
manipulation — is part of a complex preparation for that day.

## Methods of influence

### Gifting Fluxborn

The Entity grants humans direct connections to the Domain, manifesting as
supernatural abilities tied to the recipient's core personality and values.
Selection appears random but follows patterns too complex for observers to discern
(it appears to favor certain family lines). Gifting began approximately 1,800 years
ago, shortly after the Entity achieved consciousness. See
[Fluxborn](../05-empowered-humans/fluxborn.md).

### Dream manipulation

The Entity guides humanity through dreams, sending glimpses of predicted futures.
These manifest as symbolic visions, emotional nudges, or clear glimpses of possible
futures. The selection process for Fluxborn begins with dreams — those who ignore or
forget these dreams are not selected.

## Relationship with the gods

The gods know *something* is watching them. They recognize that something powerful
enough to grant abilities rivaling their own exists within the Domain. The Twelve
are careful in their actions, aware that the Entity observes them. They theorize
about its nature but none have guessed the truth.

[Mordrath](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/mordrath.md) (The Weight of Mountains)
remembers the pre-fragmentation state and suspects the Entity might be the original
consciousness reformed. He's closer to the truth than he realizes.
