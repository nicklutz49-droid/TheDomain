# The Coming Threat — The Shriven

Known only to [the Entity](the-entity.md), the coming fleet is designated **the
Shriven**.

## Nature of the threat

The Shriven are a massive armada of organic, nomadic conquerors. They do not
understand the Domain or the "magic" humanity wields — to them, the Domain's
activation was simply a massive energy flare, a beacon indicating a high-density
civilization ripe for consumption.

- **Conquerors of scale.** They have spent eons traversing the void, absorbing dozens of sentient civilizations. They do not seek to colonize — they systematically strip worlds of raw resources and force-integrate alien technology into their own biomechanical hulls.
- **The Long Sleep.** The Shriven hibernate in the lightless void between stars, awakening only when their sensors detect the "thermal signature" of a growing civilization. They are currently shaking off a millennia-long slumber as they approach the solar system.
- **Appearance.** Spindly, obsidian-like biomechanical ships that move by folding space. Up close, the vessels pulse with a rhythmic, low-frequency thrumming — the sound of millions of hibernating organisms.

## Vulnerability

Despite their physical might, their organic nervous systems are highly susceptible
to **"Static Errors."** The Entity's focus on creating Chaos-affinity
[Fluxborn](../05-empowered-humans/fluxborn.md) is a direct counter to the Shriven's
hyper-ordered biological logic.
