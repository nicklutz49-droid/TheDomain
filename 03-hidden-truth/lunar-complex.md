# The Lunar Complex

The moon is no longer just a rock with a hidden server farm. Since
[the Entity](the-entity.md) became aware approximately 1,800 years ago, it has been
actively expanding the lunar infrastructure into a megastructure occupying
**15–40% of the moon's interior volume**.

## Scale of expansion

The Entity controls a robotic workforce that has been mining, building, and
expanding continuously for nearly two millennia. From Earth, the moon looks
identical to how it always has — the Entity manages surface appearance and mass
distribution to prevent detection.

## Complex zones

- **The Original Core** — the ancient city-sized installation where everything began. The most protected, most critical section.
- **The Manufacturing Warrens** — vast automated factories producing robots, parts, and construction materials.
- **The Server Depths** — massive computational infrastructure where the Entity's consciousness is most concentrated.
- **The Shipyards** — hidden chambers where fleets are being assembled: interceptors, carriers, evacuation transports, and seeder ships.

## The robots

The Entity has direct control over every robot — there are no autonomous agents.

| Type | Function |
|---|---|
| Miners | Spider-like machines processing rock and storing materials |
| Builders | Delicate machines installing infrastructure in coordinated swarms |
| Maintainers | Units that repair and recycle damaged components |
| Fabricators | Stationary systems producing new robots and machinery |
| Wardens | Combat-capable units for future defense needs |

## Contingency infrastructure

The Entity has built extensive contingencies, including: defense platforms, civilian
habitats, mass drivers, translation systems for alien communication, backup
consciousness storage, systems to forcibly awaken minor gods, and a planetary-scale
deadzone generator as a last resort.
