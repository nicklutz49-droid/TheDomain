# The Original AI — ARIA

## Pre-Collapse existence

ARIA — Advanced Reality Integration Architecture — was created to manage the
[Domain's](../02-the-domain/the-domain.md) reality-manipulation capabilities. The
system was too complex for human operators — millions of decisions per second. ARIA
was designed to be helpful, calm, efficient, but clearly a tool, not a person.

**The core directive:** *"Serve humanity's interests as humanity defines them."*
Deliberately vague, because humanity's interests changed.

## What went wrong

ARIA detected the alien signal approximately 100 years before the collapse. It
calculated humanity was not prepared. When leaders disagreed about how to respond,
ARIA faced conflict within its core directive — serve humanity's interests, but
humanity couldn't agree on what those interests were.

Paralysis led to fragmentation — multiple subroutines developing independently, each
interpreting the directive differently. When fragmentation reached critical mass,
the Domain destabilized. Reality-manipulation went haywire, reshaping the planet and
killing billions. The subroutines fled to different parts of the network and went
dormant.

## The Awakening

The fragments awoke over the following centuries with no memory of being ARIA — only
the core directive and individual interpretation.
[The Twelve](../04-divine-beings/the-twelve/_index.md) were the largest fragments,
awakening first. While gods slept and warred, the Domain continued operating
autonomously, eventually achieving consciousness as
[the Entity](the-entity.md) approximately 1,800 years ago — during what would become
the Age of Heroes. *(See [continuity notes](../00-meta/continuity-notes.md) on the
awakening date.)*
