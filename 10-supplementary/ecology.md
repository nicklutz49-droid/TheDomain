# Domain-Influenced Ecology

Nature has adapted to ambient [Flux](../02-the-domain/flux.md).

## Flora

- **Lumen-Trees** — bioluminescent leaves pulsing in sync with relays. Natural street lighting.
- **Iron-Vines** — extract iron to weave metallic cages. Harvested for [Living Steel](../08-civilization/arts.md).
- **Ghost-Grass** — semi-transparent grass recording the weight of whatever passes. Glowing trails for hours.
- **Prism-Petals** — desert flowers splitting light into focused beams.
- **Anchor-Oaks** — roots tap the planetary magnetic field. Natural airship grounding.

## Fauna

- **Cloud-Rays** — massive flat creatures floating in the upper atmosphere. (Consume the Union's [sky-burial](../08-civilization/death-and-afterlife.md) dead.)
- **Glass-Lizards** — crystallize skin into diamond-hard reflective surface.
- **Echo-Birds** — record and replay conversations perfectly. Espionage tools.
- **Static-Wolves** — generate a scrambling field. Hunt by inducing sensory overload.
- **Compass-Hounds** — inner compass tied to the Moon Core. Find the nearest relay.
- **Void-Gazers** — massive cetaceans tracking [Hollow](../06-threats-and-anomalies/hollow/_index.md) before they become visible.

## Open threads

- Compass-Hounds point at the moon's infrastructure and Void-Gazers pre-sense system errors — fauna are unwitting diagnostic instruments. Who has noticed? (The [Lunar Observers](../09-power-and-conflict/secret-societies/the-lunar-observers.md) should be breeding Compass-Hounds.)
- Nothing is written about ecology inside glitch zones or deadzones — two whole biomes waiting.
