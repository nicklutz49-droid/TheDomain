# Pilgrimage Traditions

Relay pilgrimage traditions date to ~2,050 years ago — among the oldest surviving
institutions. See [Religion](../08-civilization/religion.md) for why
[relays](../02-the-domain/infrastructure.md) are sacred.

## The routes

- **Temple Circuit** — visiting major relay sites across one's home power. 14–20 months.
- **Grand Pilgrimage** — visiting sites across all three powers. 4–7 years. **Fewer than half complete it.**
- **Glitch Zone Pilgrimages** — entering corrupted zones to repair damaged relays.

## Support network

Pilgrim hostels, volunteer guards, professional guides. (The
[Pilgrims mercenary company](../09-power-and-conflict/mercenary-companies.md) does
the same relay-repair work for pay.)
