# Prophecies and Sacred Texts

## Major traditions

- **The Concordance Scrolls** — foundational divine rules. Missing sections supposedly contain predictions.
- **The Dream Archive (Union)** — catalogued prophetic dreams. ~3% contain verifiable predictive content. *(GM note: the Entity guides through dreams — the Archive is unknowingly a log of [Entity](../03-hidden-truth/the-entity.md) transmissions.)*
- **The Backward Saint's Testimonies** — statements from someone experiencing time in reverse. Has mentioned she *"will be there when it all begins."*
- **The Silence Prophecies** — pre-Domain predictions from [the Silence](../07-geography/the-silence.md):

  > "The stars will send their hunger. The moon will send its children. The deep
  > will send its knowledge. Only together do they survive."

  *(GM note: reads as Shriven / lunar fleet / Silence tech — a pre-collapse source
  predicting the setting's endgame. How it could predate the Domain is an open
  mystery.)*

## Forbidden texts

- **The Malevictus Fragments** — partial truths about divine nature from the sealed [Malevictus](../04-divine-beings/sealed-gods/malevictus.md). Reading without preparation causes psychological damage.
- **Axiom-1's Final Statement** — *"Look at the moon. Look at the moon. Look at the moon."* ([Axiom-1](../04-divine-beings/sealed-gods/axiom-1.md))
