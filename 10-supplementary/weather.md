# Weather Phenomena

## Managed weather

Functional relays create stable, predictable patterns (see
[the stabilizing effect](../02-the-domain/the-domain.md)). Gods can influence
weather in their territories.

## Domain storms

- **Flux Storms** — purple or gold lightning. Temporary ability disruption.
- **Reality Squalls** — localized reality distortion. Shelter in place.
- **Dream Weather** — rain falling upward, warm snow, wind carrying whispered voices.
