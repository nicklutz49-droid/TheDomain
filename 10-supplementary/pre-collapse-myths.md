# Myths of the Pre-Collapse Era

What humanity remembers of the world before — graded against the
[hidden truth](../03-hidden-truth/aria.md).

## The Paradise Lost

Perfect harmony before the collapse.
**Part true** — technology addressed material needs. **Part fantasy** — plenty of
conflict existed.

## The Great Sin

Humanity punished for reaching too high.
**Part true** — human actions caused the collapse. **Fantasy** — it was technical
failure, not moral punishment.

## The Sleeping Builders

Those who built the pre-collapse world sleep in hidden places.

*(Canon doesn't grade this one true or false. Antarctic Chaos has something stopped
in time; the Silence may hide pre-collapse works; the moon holds the Original Core.
The myth has too many candidate truths — which is what makes it dangerous.)*

## Open threads

- Myth-grading is a GM superpower: every myth is a partially corrupted memory of Part III. New myths should follow the pattern — true premise, wrong mechanism.
- Who curates these myths? The Empire, Union, and Confederacy must each tell them differently — untapped cultural texture.
